National Achievements: 

  •  Have trained more than 500 of the leaders in the Palestinian Society in issues concerning the environment among the students, teachers, farmers, households, women, and other target groups.

  • Publishing, preparing and writing the book the IBA (Important Bird Areas) in Palestine.

  • Been a part in the new curricula committee-Ministry of Education- that has to deal with the environmental conservation and education for grades from 7th to 10th.

  • Part of the Biodiversity Strategy Action Plan with the Ministry of Environmental Affairs during the years of 1997-1999.  

  • Environmental Education Activities: covering different schools- public, private and ANRWA- in the area of West Bank and Gaza through our leaders or other NGO's or GO's in the areas.

  • Writing, publishing and designing different publications that serves the best interest of the communities, the nature and the environment.
