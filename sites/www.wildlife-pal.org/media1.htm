Background of the project:

  This project, that serves the benefit of the communities of Palestine, is an attempt to help them to get “in touch” with their environment and think of better ways to conserve it and protect it. Hence, Palestine Wildlife Society (PWLS) is to produce and transmit 20 Episodes on local TV and Radio stations, as well as 20 environmental articles and reports in a local and well known newspaper.

 

Objectives of the Project:

  The project aims to develop and interaction and an “environmental relationship” with the local communities, so that to help introducing the concepts of conserving the nature and conserving the environment by covering most of the ecological problems facing their environment and discussing the best solution to avoid them, such as pollution/ Air, food, soil & water shortage.

  This is done by:

Ø      The production of 20 TV and Radio episodes on the issue of Environmental Awareness (Including Environmental Issues, Nature Conservation).

Ø      Newspaper weekly articles, Environmental advertisements and monthly reports.

   

Outputs:

 

Output 1: Production and Transmission of 20 episodes for TV.

  Activity:

This output consists of editing, producing and transmitting TV episodes that cover different environmental issues, which are important and benefit the target group. Each episode (1 and a half hour long) includes the participation of different people who have experience and knowledge on the subject that is going to be discussed, as well as 2-3 reports that give a complete coverage and a picture of the problem in the real life and the opinion of the local communities.  Each episode is transmitted live on the local TV station (Al-Mahed TV Station), which gives the chance for the viewers (from both, Local People and Responsible and Experienced people) to participate by calling and giving their opinions and suggestions.
