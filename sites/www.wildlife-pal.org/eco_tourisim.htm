Objectives:

  • To promote sustainable use of natural & cultural values of Natural and Cultural Heritage sites in Palestine.

  • To Help in conservation  and monitoring Nature and Cultural HeritageSites in Palestine.

  • Build up local capacity in conservation & Eco Tourism.

  • Promote Eco Tourism & other sustainable development program in Palestine.


Activities:

Promote sustainable use of natural & cultural values of the Natural and Cultural Heritage sites in Palestine,

  • Raise awareness within various stakeholders on the important of Natural and Cultural Heritage sites in Palestine.
  • Produce interpretation materials promoting cultural and natural values of the sites (including posters & booklets).
  • Hold workshops and seminars for the promotion of the sites and their important values.
Build up Local capacity in Eco Tourism.
  • Hold workshops & training session build up capacity of Governmental & NGO's on Eco Tourism.
  • Train & build up capacity of local NGO's & GO's on management & conservation of the sites.

The Outputs:
  1. Build up local capacity on Eco Tourism.
  2. Implementation of Biodiversity, Wildlife & UNESCO and other Conventions.
  3. Strengthening of Palestinian Biodiversity Country study and strategy.
