PWLS Multi Media Section

    The main idea of the project that is stressed over in this program, is to produce environmental & natural documentaries and other educational aids that would be distributed locally and regionally, and serve the best benefit of the environmental, Wildlife Species, human beings…etc

    Palestine Wildlife Society, since it was established in 1999, had provided the Palestinian Society with more than 6 documentaries films covering different issues such as the Flora and fauna especially  the Wildlife and Biodiversity of Wadi Gaza and Jordan Valley-Jericho, Jerusalem Wilderness/Bani Naeem Biodiversity , Birds of Palestine and still Producing other films.

PWLS Multi Media Section Staff

►     The target group: this project will target all the portions of the community. Specifically, the students, teachers, households, farmers, blue and white working classes, GO’s and NGO’s, each would be approached according to the situation and the need.

 

►     Goal and Purpose: the goal is to give the locals and national communities an overview and issues about the environment in a clear pleasurable way that can be understood by all. This program is supposed to play a major role in the lives of these communities and change their beliefs and attitudes about the environment.

 

►     This Project is considered as assists to PWLS, since it is about

                 Establishing a Multimedia Section. But the outcomes and production

                 of this Project is supposed to be ongoing to sustain the stability of it.
