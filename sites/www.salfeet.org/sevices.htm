We Have Always New Services..

 

  • The municipality provides water and electricity to the city inhabitants .

  • Issuing building permits according to the City Master Plan, Detailed Plans and the related regulations.

  •  Conserving health and environment through collecting garbage, wastes and cleaning the city. In addition, the municipality supervises hygienically different places , stores and other facilities.

  •   The municipality issues licenses for professions and trade.

The municipality has recently actualized the Public Library in order to offer services of culture , science , information and recreation to the local community.
