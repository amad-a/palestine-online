Drama Teachers' Training

We are into the second year of this two-year course, which aims at creating a pool of drama teachers, and their trainers, for Palestinian high-schools. In doing so, we are replacing our own direct involvement in-school, creating job opportunities for our trainees and helping to improve the standard and availability drama teaching in this country and beyond. It is the first program of its kind in Palestine, and has generated great involvement and interest from the whole community. There is a run down of the curriculum of this program and our evaluation of it so far in "Walking in our Footsteps"
