Comment

Arabs must possess nuclear capability for survival


The Pakistani nuclear tests, which were carried out two months ago in reaction to similar Indian tests that took place two weeks earlier, provided a glimmer of hope for a more secure future for hundreds of millions of Muslims in the Middle East and West Asia. Some people even opine with little exaggeration that the tests were the most important development in the Muslim world since the downfall of the Ottoman State in 1924.

It is true that the lethal and destructive nature of the bomb is inherently incompatible with the lofty ideals of Islam, which prefer life over death and construction over destruction. However, since we live in reality and not in fantasy, and since the ethics of brutal force prevail over the ethics of morality, it is politically and morally imperative that Muslims possess the necessary means to protect themselves against the haunting spectre of annihilation, either at the hands of the malignant and pugnacious Zionist entity, called Israel, or any other actual or potential predators.

This "dangerous vulnerability" to a prospective nuclear holocaust by the Zionists is not a phobia or an exaggerated fear of a nonexistent danger on the part of Arab-Muslim masses. It is rather a plainly real fear and therefore justified anxiety, as amply demonstrated by the wanton Zionist threats against Muslims in Lebanon and Palestine. Indeed, what guarantees does a given Muslim country, say Egypt or Iran, have against the possibility of being hit by an Israeli nuclear bomb? The answer is none.

In the New (American) World Order, the United Nations has virtually become just another tool of American foreign policy. And the United States itself happens to be in the throes of Jewish domination, given the fact that all influential decision makers in the Clinton Administration (with the exception of Clinton himself) are ardent Zionist Jews. The Congress of the United States is more responsible to the Jewish lobby than to the American people.

So, no responsible Arab or Muslim leader should be too gullible or too stupid to expect the American monkey to shield the Muslim masses from the real nuclear threats of the Zionist organ-grinder. Therefore, we must not be oblivious of the possibility that someday the Zionists would drop an atomic bomb or two on an Arab or Muslim capital, incinerating hundreds of thousands of Muslim men, women and children. How would the international community react to such a possibility-turned-reality? The answer lies in the numerous UN resolutions against Israeli aggression and the countless times the U.S. used its veto power to shield Zionist insolence.

This means that Muslims must strive to achieve a state of deterrence vis-à-vis Israel, through some form of "mutual assured destruction" or "MAD."

The successful Pakistani efforts to level with India deserve our admiration and support. Indeed, it is quite admirable for a poor third world Muslim country with limited natural resources to challenge American-Israeli hegemony and successfully carry out five nuclear tests. The Pakistani feat is a striking example of how the dignity of Muslims ought to be upheld irrespective of variable political and economic calculations. Undoubtedly, the Pakistanis will suffer due to American and European sanctions. However, hostile countries, some of which are dead set on destroying Muslim Pakistan, will now think twice before extending the hand of aggression on Pakistan.

As for the Arab world, if indeed such a thing exists at all at least in the political sense, leading Arab countries such as Egypt must give more attention to the Israeli nuclear threat next door. The conventional arms, including the state-of-the-art modern fighter planes, are never a match for Israel's nuclear arsenal. Even chemical and biological weapons are no deterrent to the Israeli nuclear arsenal. Only a threat in-kind would match the Israeli nuclear threat. Any other argument is only a waste of time.

The acquisition of a nuclear capability by Arabs and Muslims, a matter of national survival, should not be an impossibility. The technology itself is no longer enigmatic or confined to a small set of countries. Indeed, if the poor Pakistanis could do it, why can't the Arabs and the Iranians?

The possession of a nuclear capability by Muslims in the Middle East would not bring Armageddon closer. Quite the contrary, it would lessen the dangers of war by providing a counterbalance to the Israeli nuclear arsenal. This system of "MAD" worked for 40 years of cold war between the West and the former Soviet Union, and there is no reason why it can't work in the Middle East now. Hence, the bottom line is that Arabs and Muslims in this region must choose between continuing to live in the shadow of Israeli atomic bombs or developing their own deterrent. Unfortunately, there doesn't seem to be a viable third alternative.
