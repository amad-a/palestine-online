About Yabous Background Board of Trustees Staff Chart

Yabous Productions, a Palestinian organization, which was initially founded in April 1995 but officially registered as a non-profit organization in April 1997. Yabous strives to promote the artistic and cultural activities in Palestine as whole and, in particular, attempts to create a specific Palestinian cultural ambiance in Jerusalem.

Aims & Objectives:

As an organization, Yabous has agreed a set of aims and objectives which guide its work:

  • To encourage innovation in arts production

  • The events will happen in areas where there is little or no existing activity

  • Reflects the needs and interests of our audience

  • Yabous strives to promote the artistic and cultural activities in Palestine as a whole and, in particular, attempts to create a specific Palestinian cultural ambiance in Jerusalem

  • Yabous will place special emphasis on cross cultural exchange by presenting  a variety of international attributes to Palestine and by promoting Palestinian productions locally and abroad

Policy

  • To develop an interactive organization based in Jerusalem

  • To present productions of quality which stimulate, entertain, excite and appeal to audience

  • To attract touring shows of quality drawn from all areas of theatre, dancing, music and other associated art forms

  • To attract and develop audience

  • To ensure that the spirit of innovation, development and professionalism plays a full part in organization's activities

  • to ensure that the organization plays its part in the cultural, educational and economic life of the city, region and hope in the nation
