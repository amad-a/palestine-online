Yabous Productions

Aims & Objectives Background Board of Trustees Staff Chart

Yabous Productions is a Jerusalem based non profit organization aiming to revive and enrich the cultural life in Palestine with particular emphasis on Jerusalem.

The organization is run by a qualified team and its activities coordinated by a Board of Trustees.

Yabous is considered as the pioneer organization in the cultural field in Palestine.

"Our aim is to continue to make Jerusalem more interesting, enjoyable, accessible and responsive to our public and people."
