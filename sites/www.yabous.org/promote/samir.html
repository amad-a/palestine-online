Samir Jubran
Oud Player

Education Highlights of Qualification
Participations Technical Rider Position on Stage

Education:

High School Degree, 1990

Salvatorian Sisters School, Nazareth

Diploma of Arts with emphasis on Oud, 1993

Abdul-Wahab Conservatoire for Eastern Music, Cairo

Highlights of Qualification:

  • Well-organized, strong in planning and implementing special events and festivals

  • Effective in management, leadership and managerial responsibilities

  • Competent in lecturing about Eastern Music History

  • Qualified in training of the Oud instrument and Music Theory

Participations:

  • 1998, Technical Manager of a Sketch that was made for the 50th Memory of the 1948 Disaster (Nakba)

  • 1997, Produced a musical CD named "Taqasim"

  • 1997, Participated with the famous Palestinian poet Mahmoud Darwish in the opening session of Jerash Festival (Jordan): A Live Poetry - Music Performance

  • 1997, Participated in the Palestinian Creative Festival in France and was invited to give Workshops and Live Music performance in Dankerk

  • 1997, Solo Oud Concert in the Arab World Institute in Paris

  • 1997, Performed in conjunction with the famous Palestinian poet Mahmoud Darwish in Exprovance

  • 1996, Accompanied the famous poet Samih Al Qasem in poetry readings in and outside Palestine with two significant events for Palestinian Heritage in Al-Doha, Qatar and the Arab Book Fair in Cairo

  • 1994, Founded Al'Een Nazareth Group which participated in the 3rd Arabic Musical Festival in the Opera House in Cairo

  • 1993, Was delegated to represent Palestine in the Arabic Music Festival in the Opera House in Cairo in a Solo Oud performance in the second Arabic Festival

  • 1993, Honored by the Abdul-Wahhab Conservatoire to perform on the occasion of the anniversary of Muhammad Al-Qasabj's 100th birthday, which was held in the Arabic National Theatre (George Abyad)

  • December 25, 1992 was invited by the Maestro Salim Sahaab to give a solo performance in the Opera House in Cairo

  • 1992, Participated in commemorative celebration for the late composer Muhammad Abdul-Wahhab in the Abdul-Wahhab Conservatoire in Cairo

  • 1994-1998, Taught Eastern Music and Oud in private institutes and schools.
