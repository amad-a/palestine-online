 Promoting Local Artists and Groups

With all the efforts accomplished by our Palestinian musicians and groups to promote themselves locally and internationally - their time is limited. They are involved more in composing, practicing and performing and someone has to do the job.

With our experience and our staff's knowledge as a production company, festival and concert organizers, we will try to promote some Palestinian groups and to assure a musical enrichment for the audience as well as excellent and unforgettable experience arrangement for the musicians.

We also work on the program arrangements tours, airline tickets if needed, venue preparation, technical requirements, insurance, payments, health and safety measures etc.
