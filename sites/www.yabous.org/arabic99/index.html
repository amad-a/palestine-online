The Arabic Music Festival 1999

July 1-10/1999

The Arabic Music Festival is an annual event. Once again it will be held at the Tombs of the Kings. The great historical place with rich traditional architectural, seats over 800 people.

The Festival provides an opportunity for local and international Arabic groups to perform their best quality of their musical experience.

This year we will host very well known musicians from the World and Palestine under the theme: Developing cultural links between our country and the rest of the Arab World, as well as displaying the diverse and rich treasure of our land Palestine.

Alongside all the concerts, the artists will hold daytime workshops, in collaboration with the students and teachers of the National Conservatory of Music and with local Palestinian Musicians.
