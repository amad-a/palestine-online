The Jerusalem Jazz Festival 1999

March 18 - 24/1999

Click to view program

For this festival, our goal was to host six consecutive nights of Jazz during March 18-23, 1999. The main events took place in Jerusalem at the YMCA (300 capacity) in East Jerusalem. We also held additional concerts by the performing artists in Ramallah and Bethlehem where a large audience attended. Alongside the concerts, the artist held daytime workshops, in collaboration with The National Conservatory of Music, with local Palestinian musicians that introduced them to the big and beautiful world of Jazz.

1999 Jazz Festival Program
18 of March 1999 Opening night: Bradley Williams & his original 21Century Review - U.S.A

19 of March 1999 The Brasshoppers- United Kingdom

20 of March 1999 Sigi Finkel & African Heart - Austria

22 of March 1999 Franck Amsallem - France

23 of March 1999 Magnus Lindgren Quartet & Rigmor Gustafsson - Sweden

24 of March 1999 Closing night: Transit - Palestine

Ticket price: 120 NIS for all performances Or for six persons per one performance. All performances were held at the YMCA- Nablus Road - Jerusalem

Additional Performances:

Ramallah-20/3/99: Bradley Williams and his group
Bethlehem- 22/3/99: Sigi Finkel & African Heart
