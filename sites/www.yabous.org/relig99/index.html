The Religious and Soufi Music Festival 1999

December 25/1999-January 5/2000

I n December 1997 the First Suffi Music Festival was held in Jerusalem. This year the Religious Music Festival is sure to be the highlight of our coming 2000! Since Yabous has created a special program on the occasion of the Holy month - Ramadan & Christams 2000 with concerts, workshops and praise.

We have to admit that this festival must be the most important and largest event of its type in the world. Why? Because it is held in Palestine the place which is celebrating the Beth 2000 and Ra madan in the same month. And also because of the spiritual impact it has in the lives of millions of people.

Join us for excitement packed days of non-stop music inspired. Were choral groups and musicians will perform at a variety of venues in the mosques and churches. And in two great locations, in Bethlehem and in Jerusalem. Groups from Morocco, Norway, Turkey, Austia, Greece and United Kingdom will participate in this Festival.
