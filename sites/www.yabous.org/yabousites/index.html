 Yabousites Evenings

October 14th - December 2nd, 1999

The Yabousites Evenings: Musical, dance or theatrical evenings performed by local high caliber artists are organized periodically to expose the Palestinian cultural heritage.

Yabusites Evenings are set in cooperation with the Autumn Season in the National Conservatory of Music. We are planning to host a weekly performance, around 50 performances for this year at the YMCA, Lutheran Church and other venues. Groups are invited from Palestine and inside the green line in order to revive the cultural life in Jerusalem.
