Car Rentals & Taxis

  October 27, 1999: this section is not ready yet. Please check back in the next few days. If you'd like us to add your car rental or taxi office number, please don't hesitate to contact us (02-234-3379)
Getting around for tourists in Palestine is easy if you get used to the transportation system. Public transportation is available from early morning to late evening and it doesn't cost much. There are also tax services if you prefer and they are affordable as well. If you'd like to drive on your own (and you better be familiar with the driving culture here), there are many car rental companies with offices in all major cities.
Remember to get a yellow plated car if you plan to go to Jerusalem or areas under Israeli control.
