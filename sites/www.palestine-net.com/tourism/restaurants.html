  No matter what your eating habits and preferences are, be assured that you'll feel home in Palestine. In addition to our mild, nutritious, tasty and relatively inexpensive dishes (like Falafel, Hummous, Shawerma, Kebab, Mansaf or Kunafa sweets), you can find most ethnic cuisines at restaurants in major cities and near tourist attractions.
  Fast food is available almost anywhere (especially falafel and shawerma sandwishes). Most Internet cafe's provide internet access, some snacks and drinks.
