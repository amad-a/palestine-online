  Palsoft is one of the major Internet cafe's in Ramallah, featuring a lounge with 20 Internet workstations running Win98. Two units have 17" monitors, the rest are 15". The cafe offers printing (b/w laser, color and color copies). A scanner is available as well.
Palsoft connects via a direct line to an ISP offering from 64 K to 1024 K and all workstations are equipped with UPS. You can get hot and cold drinks, smoking is allowed and the cafe has a W.C. (restroom) inside. All computers are multimedia ready
