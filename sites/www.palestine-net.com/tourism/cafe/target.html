  Target Internet Club is located in the heart of Nablus. It is the largest iCafe we've seen so far with 25 PCs running mainly windows 98. All workstations have15" monitors except one which is equipped with a 20" terminal. The space is split into 3 rooms, one of them is smoking free with multimedia capable machines. The Club does not currently offer drinks or snacks but these are available closeby. There are 2 W.C units (restrooms) inside.
