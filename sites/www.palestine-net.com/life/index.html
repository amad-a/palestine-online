Palestine-Net: Life in Palestine

Have you participated in the Visit Palestine campaign?

  Life in Palestine is our most recent section at Palestine-Net. It is currently an experimental step and we hope our visitors will like and encourage it with feedback and suggestions.
Through this section, we hope to conduct interviews with different people especially from the Palestinian street representing various aspects of our life. We will try to make the interviews as random and diverse as we can (or as our abilities and limits allow). This section will also contain occasional pictures from Palestine.
Some of the interviews will be in Arabic. If you find difficulty viewing Arabic text, please check out how to read Arabic on the Internet from our other site - Arabic2000.
