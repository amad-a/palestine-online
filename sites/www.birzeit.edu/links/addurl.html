Inclusion in "Complete Guide to Palestine's Websites"

The primary purpose of The Complete Guide to Palestine's Websites has always been to show which institutional and individual websites are in Palestine as opposed to about Palestine, although we are doing some work on providing links to the latter. This is due to the fact that there is an urgent developmental need to make known the progress of our regional strand of the World Wide Web. For more information about development of the World Wide Web in Palestine, please see Birzeit & the Web.

Websites about Palestine - all is not lost...

Since it's launch, the Complete Guide has extended its links to institutions and individuals whose websites deal with Palestine as an issue. If you fall into this category, please feel free to send your URL for a slower inclusion by typing "palestine-outside" into the subject box of this e-mail link, together with brief details about your website in the message body.

In the future, it is intended that an extensive directory similar to the current Complete Guide (with reviews) will be offered for Websites about Palestine, funding permitting. Adding links to Websites about Palestine is lower priority than the rest of the Birzeit website. At the moment, only website names, categories and URLs will be included, unless of course you get featured in our site/s of the month section on the Complete Guide homepage.
