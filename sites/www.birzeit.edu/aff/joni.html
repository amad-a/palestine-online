The portraits displayed in this website were taken from an exhibition by photographer Joni Lane who visited Birzeit University during the autumn of 1995. Joni Lane is currently based in Boston where she is studying for her masters in fine arts (MFA) in photography at the Massachusetts College of Art.

For more information about hosting the exhibition or
ordering photographs, contact Joni Lane directly..
