right to education

The form below will allow you to send a letter to the Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, to raise the issue of Gaza students right to education at the university of their choice and calling on Mr. Netanyahu to ensure this right by adopting clear and standardized procedures which will enable Gaza students to move freely to and from the university of their choice. A copy is sent automatically to the organisers of the Gaza Students Campaign. You may either use the existing text, modify it, or write your own letter. Short, politely-worded letters have been proven most effective. Those wishing to write by normal mail or send a fax should do so through the following address:
