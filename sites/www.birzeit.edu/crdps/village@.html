Notes

  • The use of the term "Zionist" in this section of the website arises as a historical necessity, and not from any political agenda. The term "Israel" or "Israeli" cannot always be used since many of the events described here took place before the foundation of the State of Israel (15 May 1948).
  • The terms nahiya () and liwa' () are Ottoman administrative terms. A liwa' was a mid-sized administrative district made up of several smaller districts called nahiya.

If you are interested in acquiring any of the monographs about the villages contained in this site, please contact the Center at the address listed below. All the monographs are available in Arabic, and several are available in English.
