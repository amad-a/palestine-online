Architecture Engineering Department



 
 

Faculty Position


The Department of Architecture at Birzeit  University invites applications for faculty positions  starting Fall Semester 1999/2000. Applicants should have Ph.D. or M.Sc. degrees in Architectural Engineering. Preferred applicants should have their research or teaching experience in the areas of Architectural Design, Theories of Architecture, Urban Design and Planning .

A complete application shall consist of a covering letter, curriculum vitae, list of publications, summary of teaching and work experience, two reference letters, a copy of graduate and undergraduate transcripts.
