Palestinian National Unity Against Israeli Atrocities

With a cannibalistic desire to see Palestinians kill each other, the arrogant Israeli premier Benyamin Netanyahu has been issuing incessant demands, urging Palestinian Authority Chairman Yasser Arafat to "fight the terrorists and destroy their social, educational and health infrastructure" before the peace process can be resumed. For anybody with minimal knowledge of Israeli phraseology, this is an unmistakable allusion to orphanages, nursing homes, hospices, hospitals, kindergartens and mosques that are run by Islamic elements. So Benyamin Netanyahu, whose soldiers routinely deny Palestinian women in labour access to nearby hospitals, would like to see tens of thousands of poor Palestinians thrown into the streets. That is, of course, in addition to rounding up hundreds, if not thousands, of Islamic activists who have not done anything wrong save refusing to surrender to Netanyahu's murderous insolence and arrogance of power.

Netanyahu's morbid demands suggest that the racist Israeli leader classifies the Palestinians into two categories: collaborators, viewed by the ìsuperiorî Jewish race as slaves and a slave can't be a chooser; and terrorists, who ought to be mercilessly wiped out. Hence, every Palestinian who's political thinking is incompatible with Netanyahu's whims of grandeur is considered a terrorist that ought to be liquidated or at least kept behind bars for as long as it takes to mollify Netanyahu's bloody mood. Along the same lines, the Israeli leader seems to believe that the legal maxim "a person is innocent unless proven guilty" does not apply to Palestinians.

Fortunately, Mr. Arafat has not succumbed to Netanyahu's wishes to see doom and gloom ravage the Palestinian society and eventually weaken or destroy its collective ability to extract Palestinian rights from Israel's criminal hands. Arafat must have realized that the terrorism issue is but a smokescreen used by a truly terrorist Israeli government to conceal the repugnant reality of Israeli policies and practices in the West Bank and East Jerusalem, including the demolition of homes, land confiscation, ethnic cleansing and apartheid.

Moreover, the Palestinian leader has apparently come to the conclusion that Netanyahu is after him personally and after his Authority, as much as he is after Hamas, the Islamic Jihad and other nationalist Palestinian groups opposed to Israeli occupation and domination. So, the evil design goes, instead of Israel herself doing the genocidal task, the Jewish state should do it vicariously or through a proxy, e.g. by coercing Arafat and his PA into killing and being killed by their Islamist compatriots. Then, God forbid, Netanyahu and the rest of his gang would gleefully claim "Look! Arabs are killing Arabs. We have nothing to do with it," the very same remarks they uttered when Lebanese Christian militiamen butchered hundreds of defenseless Palestinian refugees at the Sabra and Shatilla refugee camps in Lebanon in September 1982.

The Palestinian people must put up an effective response to Netanyahu's savagery and evil by denying him the opportunity to see us kill each other. The strife is after all between Israel (the oppressor) and us, all of us (the oppressed), and Netanyahu and the rest of the Zionist establishment do not distinguish between a member of Fatah and a member of Hamas because the good Arab is "the dead Arab" in their book. We must therefore strengthen our national unity; the bedrock of our continued survival. This can be achieved not only by dialogue forums here and there but essentially adopting a comprehensive and cohesive strategy aimed at delivering the Palestinian people from the cancerous shackles of the Israeli occupation.

Indeed, if Arafat can't undo Oslo, he shouldn't; the Palestinians must not allow Oslo to undo Palestinian rights. A brave and responsible stance to that effect by the Palestinian Authority would undoubtedly invite American government consternation and indignation. But the U.S. Administration, the premier tormentor of our people, has always connived with the Zionist entity against our rights, which should make us un-obliged to meet her oblique political calculations.
