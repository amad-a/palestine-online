A Revolutionary-Turned-Quisling

The Palestinian Authority (PA) is said to have given its consent to a new "security coordination pact" with Israel under the supervision of the US Central Intelligence Agency (CIA).

According to the Israeli Yediot Aharonot daily newspaper, the security pact consists of 13 provisions made up by the CIA. It includes the cooperation between the PA and Israel in fighting "terrorism", especially the trading of information about suspects.

The pact virtually enables the Zionist State to determine who among the Palestinians within the autonomous enclaves ought to be interned, how long he or she must languish in jail, and if and when detainees would be released.

Moreover, the new manifest disgrace would, inter alia, oblige the PA to forward to the CIA a list of Palestinian detainees to be released beforehand. The CIA's representative in Israel would then relay the information to the Israeli intelligence.

In addition, the PA would have to exercise "tighter control" on the mosques, schools, universities, charitable institutions and other bodies where the Islamists have influence.

This could mean that "critics (e.g. mosque speakers) of the Israeli occupation and terrorism will be silenced."

It could even mean, given past PA disgraceful caving in to Israeli-American dictates, that Imams and Muslim scholars citing and interpreting Quranic verses critical of the oppression and evil perpetrated by Zionists against Palestinians and other Arabs (e.g. South Lebanon) will be asked to refrain from using the Muslim holy book in their Friday sermons.

Embarrassed by the scandalous terms of the agreement, PA officials have so far sought to extenuate the stunning gravity and shocking perfidy of the document, saying that what it contained was nothing new.

However, no Palestinian official has flatly denied Israeli and foreign reports to that effect (the Palestinian press is heavily censored and hence did not report on the subject).

The security cooperation between the self-rule Authority's security services and the Israeli counterpart is not new. Many Palestinians do not know that the Oslo Accords are themselves "security" in nature. The year 1997 witnessed close cooperation between the two parties. The information given by the PA's Preventive Security Service to the Israeli Shin Bet led to the arrest of the "Surif" cell, said to be responsible for a number of military attacks against Israeli targets in Occupied Palestine. On 13 November 1997, the PSS also handed over two members of the Surif cell to Israeli security officers. The two members of the military wing of Hamas have been held by the PA security for several months.

The newest development in the recent security pact is the direct intervention by the CIA, which plays the role of "coordinator" between the PA and Israeli security bodies.

The PA rationale behind signing the treacherous agreement is ostensibly to induce Israel to carry out the second phase of redeployment in the West Bank.

However, Israeli and American officials have stressed that there is no linkage between the "security subject" and the "political talks over redeployment."

In light of the above, it is amply safe to conclude that the agreement is "a self-evident act of grand treason committed by an authority which claims to represent the aspirations of the Palestinian people for freedom and liberation from Zionist shackles and domination.

How else can one possibly describe placing the entire Palestinian civil society hostage to Israel's security whims?

Now, as it is sufficiently clear, even the fig leaf has been removed, exposing an entity even more deformed and ugly than it formerly appeared­a quisling body of "collaborators" striving to serve the enemy.

Netanyahu will not give Arafat anything in return. In other words, Arafat and his moribund Authority will achieve nothing except an image of treachery and perfidy engraved in the individual and collective consciousness of the Palestinian people.

Soon, perhaps very soon, there will come a time when the traitors are torn from within, sterile and depressive, with nothing to live for. In any case, they will get what they deserve.
