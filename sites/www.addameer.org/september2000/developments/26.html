URGENT:Latest Developments No. 26 - Thursday 12 October 2000,Web posted 12:30pm (GMT+2), Ramallah, West Bank

Three Israelis it is not known if undercover units or settlers were apprehended by Palestinian police in center of Ramallah near the Ramallah Boys Friends School. They were carrying explosives. The three were beaten by protestors and their car was burnt. Palestinian police have advised residents to evacuate the center of Ramallah and 4 Israeli Apache Cobra helicopters are currently circling low over the centre of the city.
