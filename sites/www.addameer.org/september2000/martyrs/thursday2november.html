Martyrs Thursday 2 November 2000
  Khaled Al Khateeb, 17 years old from Hizma, Jerusalem, was shot and killed today by live ammunition by one of the settlers who attacked Palestinian school children on their way to school early in the morning.
  'Adli 'Ubeid, 21 years old from Shate' (Beach) Refugee Camp, died of injuries sustained from the day before by live ammunition at Mentar Crossing, Gaza.
  Yazan Mohhamad Issa Halayqa, 19 years old from Hebron, was shot and killed today by live ammunition in Al Khader, Beithlehem.
