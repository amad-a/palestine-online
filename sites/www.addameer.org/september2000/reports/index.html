  • Added 24 November, 2000:
    Addameer Report on Palestinian Prisoners as of 29 September 2000 - 1 November 2000 This report by Addameer Prisoners Support and Human Rights Association examines the widespread arrest campaign carried out by the Israeli military, police and security agencies following the clashes that begun in late September. The report examines the following issues:
    • Cases of Physical Abuse
    • Detention of minors
    • Prison conditions faced by Palestinian minors
    • Administrative detainees
    • The use of torture against Palestinian detainees
    • General Prison Conditions
    • Cancellation of family visits and lawyer's permits

  • Added 24 November, 2000:
    Amnesty International A report by Amnesty International entitled: Israel and the Occupied Territories: Mass Arrests and police Brutality. This report examines the issues of mass arrests and police brutality through several case studies of arrests by Israeli forces. It includes recommendations from Amnesty International.
