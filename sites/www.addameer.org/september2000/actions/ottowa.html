Ottawa-Canada Demonstration
Friday 6 October

Report from Kamal Tabaja

Over 1500 people from different contries and different political sects, Arab , Canadians and even Americas, gathered in front of the Israeli embassy, marched to Parliament Hill and then to the US embassy.

They called on the UN and the world to stop the crime, and encouraged the Arab world to fight to recover all of Palestine.

The crowd shouted verses against Israel, US & Zionism. They also burned the Israeli flag in all locations and threw stones at the US embassy.
