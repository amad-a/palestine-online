USA: A network of organizations is calling for a national week of teach-ins and public forums to discuss “The Question of Palestine” during the week of November 2-9 We are asking our chapters, activists, and local affiliates to join with one another and with other concerned organizations to hold public events, panels, speakers and films during that week. We urge all those who support the rights of Palestinians to join these events -- or to take the initiative to organize an event in your own community. This week of teach-ins is co-sponsored by: American-Arab Anti-Discrimination Committee, Christian Peacemakers Team, Fellowship of Reconciliation, Peace Action, War Resisters League, and Womens International League for Peace and Freedom. U.S. branch. Several other peace organizations are also discussing becoming co-sponsors. The current crisis makes this a good time to reach a wide public audience whose interest has been newly awakened. While there have been many local activities already, a coordinated national day of educational forums which bring together a variety of communities will have a wider impact and more likely to draw media attention. The teach-ins should address the current situation and show its roots in the more than years of occupation and the more than 50 years of dispossession. November 2 is the anniversary of the Balfour Declaration, which some may find a timely moment in which to put the current crisis into its historical perspective. In the current atmosphere, it is essential that we help people look beyond the daily violence to the history of injustice which lies at the root of the conflict. This can be an occasion for both political analysis and for the statement of grief over the loss of life and the statement of support for all those working for peace in the region. We ask organizations and individuals to reach out to one another and to the public. It is especially important to reach the media and provide alternative voices to counteract the biased and too-often anti-Arab coverage. ADC can supply a packet of information and resources to use. ADC is also working on producing a video of the recent clashes. Please join in and plan one or more events during the week of November 2-9. Please keep us informed of your activities. For more information, to obtain resource materials, or to report on your plans for events during November 2-9, please contact: Marvin Wingfield Director of Education and Outreach American-Arab Anti-Discrimination Committee (ADC) (202) 244-2990 marvinw@adc.org
