Israeli army terrorizes Palestinian village earmarked for annexation

Redress Information & Analysis http://www.redress.btinternet.co.uk

The Israeli army is continuing to terrorize the West Bank village of Hares with a view to forcing its Palestinian residents to leave and thus paving the way for the expansion of a nearby illegal Jewish settlement.

For a report by the Israeli activist Neta Golan, see http://www.redress.btinternet.co.uk/hares2.htm.
