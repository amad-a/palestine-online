LAW

Israeli Forces Kill Two Palestinians and Wound 80

Today, Israeli forces opened fire on demonstrators throughout the West Bank and Gaza. In Tulkarem, Israeli forces opened fire with heavy machine guns on a crowd of Palestinian demonstrators, killing 25- year-old Ahmed Mohammed Khalil Kasim. According to eyewitnesses, his body was riddled with bullets.

  • In Qalkilya, Bashir Saleh Mousa Shalewiyh, 15 and deaf, was shot in the stomach live ammunition.
  • In Qalandia, Ibrahim Khalil Yousef Ahmad Al Iyad, 27, was shot in the face with a rubber-coated metal bullet. He is in critical condition.
  • In Kufur Haris, rubber-coated metal bullets injured 3 Palestinians.
  • In Ramallah, rubber-coated metal bullets injured 40.
  • In Gaza, 5 were injured at the Salah el Din Gate in Rafa, 3 were hit with live ammunition. In Erez, 7 Palestinians were injured, 4 were hit with rubber-coated metal bullets fired from weapons with silencers. In Khan Younis, rubber-coated metal bullets injured 7.

One hour ago, Israeli forces stationed at the City Inn north of Ramallah, opened fire on an ambulance, shattering the windshield and severely injuring the driver in the face.

LAW

One More Dies, 65 more injured

This evening, Ghassan Yousef Salem Awayeseh 27, from A'daharaieh village was shot in the chest during clashed at the City Inn (Ramallah). He died instantly.

In Jenin, Fadi Amin Eheraiwish, 27, was shot in the head with live ammunition. He is in critical condition. Forty-three others were injured, 35 by tear gas and 8 were shot with live ammunition.

In Gaza, 15 Palestinians were injured in Khan Younis. Eight were hit with rubber-coated metal bullets, 7 with live ammunition. In Rafa, 3 were injured by tear gas and in Erez, 2 Palestinians were critically injured. Farris Attiyeh Dawas, 16 was hit by live ammunition in the pelvis and another unidentified man was shot in the stomach with live ammunition.

The ambulance driver injured when Israeli forces shot at his windshield has been identified as Shaher Abdullah.
