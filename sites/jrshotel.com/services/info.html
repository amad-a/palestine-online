Travel Information What to pack:

Summer, April – October: Lightweight suit slacks shorts and open-neck shirts, light cotton daytime dresses, light shoes, sandals and walking shoes, sunglasses and hat, swimsuit and beachwear, light coat, jacket or sweater for cool evenings in the hills.

Winter, November – March: Warm coat sweaters, raincoat, walking shoes, shirts, slacks, sport jacket suite, blouses and skirts.
