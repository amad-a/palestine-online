Room Facilities
Rooms are timelessly decorated with antiques that are inspired by eastern culture and traditions.

Each of our 14 rooms has its own character partly due to the nature of the architecture and partly because of the uniqueness of each room's furniture.

All furniture is carefully handcarved with formations inspired by early Islamic and Andalucian designs. Each bed, closet, bureau, mirror, chair and side table offer additional details that further differentiate each room.

All rooms have private facilities providing twenty four hour comfort to guests.

All rooms have two seperate telephone lines, which is particularly handy for those occupying one of the lines for internet or fax use.

All rooms have a satellite television that includes a 24 hour movie channel, TVLand, MTV, CNN and others. Video machines are available upon request.

There is no electrical air conditioning in the rooms except for a ceiling fan. However, because of the thickness of the walls (about 90 cm) and the height of the ceiling (over 4 meters), the rooms maintain quite a comfortable temperature during summer. A central heating system maintains a warm and pleasant temperature during winter.

Room balconies overlook the Mount of Olives, the Old City, the Holy Sepulchre, the Lutheran Church, the Dome of the Rock, the Russian Compound and other historic sites.
