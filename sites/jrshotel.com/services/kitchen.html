The Jerusalem Hotel Kitchen

Kan Zaman		 Kan Zaman, which means once upon a time, is the name of the Jerusalem Hotel restaurant. The restaurant is based on an a la carte service, but special banquet and gala settings can be arranged on request. Guests may enjoy our service daily from 11:00-23:00

Visit us for a unique Palestinian culinary experience

During the summer season, a carefully presented buffet of Palestinian and Lebanese delicacies is prepared every Saturday evening, associated with live music entertainment by virtuoso "Oud" player Mr. Ghidian Kaimari.
