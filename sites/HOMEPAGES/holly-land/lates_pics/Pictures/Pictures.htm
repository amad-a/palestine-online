Israeli occupation forces have committed many war crimes, including willful and extra-judicial killing, against Palestinian civilians in the Occupied Palestinian Territories. PCHR and other Palestinian and international human rights organizations have documented many of these crimes.

On Friday, 8 March 2002, in an apparent willful killing, a special Israeli police unit shot dead 22-year-old Mahmoud Sa’id Salah, from Beit Wazan village, west of Nablus, approximately half an hour after he was arrested in Beit Hanina area in occupied Jerusalem.
