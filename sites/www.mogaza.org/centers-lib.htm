 Municipality's Public Library
Overview

    Inaugurated the 1st of July 1999, the first Public Library of Gaza City is the fruit of the twining agreement between Gaza city and Urban Community of Dunkerque (France).

Open for everyone and conceived for every public, it offers to the people of Gaza a memory and a window to the world. It is not only a place of knowledge and intelligence but it also stands as a place of freedom of thoughts.

The Library has 10,000 books, 30 periodicals, over 300 cross-referenced bibliographies on selected topics and 400 children books. These books came mainly from donations: Ministry of Culture of Morocco, Arab World Institute (Paris), Abd al Hamid Shoumman foundation (Jordan), Mrs. Moubarak through the Children Association (Egypt), and other donors.

With a computerized catalogue, a multimedia room, an internet and information unit, Gaza Library is ready to answer the needs of the 3rd millennium.

The GMPL was a result of common efforts and partnership of different donors as follows:

Building: Nicely settled in the rehabilitated electricity corporation's old building (1,410 m²) , the library works were carried out with the assistance of the Urban Community of Dunkerque, Gaza Municipality, Cités Unies France, the World Bank through PECDAR, Caisse des dépôts et consignations (France).

Training of the librarians: The librarians have been trained in France and in Jordan through a partnership between: Urban Community of Dunkerque, French Ministry of Culture, Arab World Institute, Georges Pompidou Center, Consulate General of France in Jerusalem and Abd Al Hamid Shoumman Foundation (Amman).

Computerization: UNESCO, Abd Al Mouhsen Qatan foundation (London) and the Municipality of Gaza.

Internet and Information Unit: In 2005, a well equipped hall with internet and information facilities has been established with the assistance of the city of Turin and a contribution from Piemonte region through CICSENE-Pianeta Possibile
