The Mayor's Office
Director: Mr. Salah Abu Dayya

Responsibilities:

  • To receive different complaints from the residents, to try to find suitable solutions and direct them to the proper and related sections and departments.

  • To receive different official and popular delegations who come to see the Mayor.

  • To document all the work related to the office of the Mayor and his Deputy.

  • To prepare the in and out files of the office of the Mayor and his Deputy.

  • To receive all the telephone calls of the Mayor and his Deputy.

  • To organize the appointments, visits and meetings of the Mayor, with official and popular delegations.

  • To help and participate in the duties of the different committees entrusted to him by the Mayor.

  • To follow up all the correspondence of the Mayor and his Deputy.

  • To receive answers from the different sections and departments.

  • To write the letters of the Mayor and his Deputy.

  • To help the Deputy Mayor in the mail and letters from the different official and popular organizations and refer them to the related parties.

  • To give opinions and technical and legal advice to the Mayor and his Deputy.

  • To type all the letters of the Mayor, his Deputy and the General Director of the municipality.

  • To follow up the decisions of the municipal council and the committees which are formed on the bases of these decisions and document them.


Mr. Salah Abu-Dayyah
Mayor's Office Manager
