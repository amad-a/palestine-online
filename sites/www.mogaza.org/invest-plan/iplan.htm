Three Year Investment Plan and
Financing Plan in the Municipality of Gaza

July 2000

Executive Summary

 

    The Municipality of Gaza, in collaboration with PADCO, Inc., USA, prepared a Development Strategy Report for the municipality in December 1999. The Final Report that is now presented gives a comprehensive Three Year Investment Plan and a Financing Plan supported by a capacity building component. The consulting assignment for this project was funded under the Municipal Infrastructure Development Project of the World Bank.

     The Final Report has a separate appendix, titled Appendix D, that shows the engineering data and information prepared by the Technical Committee of the Municipality of Gaza. Appendix D shows detailed costing of the area upgrading component and trunk road rehabilitation.

     We would like to thank the World Bank representatives in Gaza, Jerusalem, and the Washington, DC, offices for their supportive oversight of our work. We would also like to thank the Palestine National Authority representatives as well as the representatives from the Palestinian Economic Council for Development and Reconstruction for their supportive role.
