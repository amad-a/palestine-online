Rashad Shawa Cultural Center
Objectives

 


The Rashad Shawa Center under construction, it used to be known as "The Islamic-Arabic Cultural Center"

Objectives of The Center:

  • To be the focal place from which all Palestinians may be able to direct their efforts to retaining, rebuilding and enhancing their status as people rich in culture, history and tradition.

  • To encourage productive coordination and cooperation between the Palestinian people all around the world and the international community.

  • To develop mutual understanding and respect among individuals, organizations and other groups.

  • To offer the place and opportunity for individuals and organized groups to interact positively through well organized discussions and meetings.

  • To offer services  through its modern library, books, reference materials and access to the latest information to all those who may require them.

  • To provide the opportunity for Palestinian women to develop and organize activities in which they will participate to improve their status in the community.

  • To innitiate the needed training courses, workshops and intellectual debates which will lead to more understanding, cooperation, and better working conditions between all the groups and individuals who are active in Gaza strip.

  • To cooperate with similar centers locally and internationally in coordinating activities that will help in creating understanding between Palestinians and others.
