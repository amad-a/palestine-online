UTO
express its solidarity with Palestinian People
Friday, October 6th, 2000

    A letter of solidarity was sent by UTO (United Towns Organization) for the attention of Mr. Aown Shawa, Mayor of Gaza and President of the Association of Palestinian Local Authorities ( APLA):

“At a time when the Palestinian people is going through a difficult period of existence, full of pain and sorrow, we would like to express our total solidarity. UTO firmly condemns this violence which has hit the Palestinian people with such disproportionate means.

UTO reiterates its solidarity with the Palestinian people and its support for its local elected representatives and reaffirms the right of the Palestinian people to live in peace , in an independent state, with Jerusalem as its capital”.

Signed by Michel Bescond, Secretary General of UTO.

    Mr. Shawa, Mayor of Gaza and President of APLA has received similar letters of solidarity with Palestinian people against the Israeli brutal unjustified attacks from various International organizations and European cities.
