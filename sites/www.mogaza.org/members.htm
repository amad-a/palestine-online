Ghazi M. Mushtaha
Member of the Municipal Council

  • Born in Gaza city in 1946
  • A businessman.
  • Has a diploma in Pharmacy, Pharmacist Assistant
  • Head of the Purchase Committee of the municipal council and member of other committees in the council
  • Member of the Administrative Board of the Palestine Company for Land Investment of PADICO Company
  • Member of the Administrative Board of the National Insurance Company in Ramlallah.
  • Member of the Administrative Board of the Palestine Med-lab Company for Diagnostic Centers and Medical Laboratories
  • Member of the Administrative Board of the Union of Industrialists Society, Gaza Strip
  • Head of the Administrative Board of the Al-Arusa Ice Cream Company, Gaza
  • Head of the Administrative Board of the Medica Company for Medicines
  • Head of the Administrative Board of the Maxim Company for Tourism
  • Share holder of and participant in establishing several investment companies and Palestinian economic organizations.

Issa N. Tarazi
Member of the Municipal Council

  • Head of the Sports Committee of the municipal council
  • Graduate of Najah University, 1973 with a major in Science and Mathematics
  • Vice-president of the Administrative Board of Al-Zaytoon Sports Club and an active member of the sports movement in Gaza Strip
  • Head of the Administrative Board of the Universal Agency for Tourism and Travel

Abdel Fattah Humeid
Member of the Municipal Council

  • Has a B. A. degree in Commercial Sciences from Egypt, 1972
  • Worked as a teacher for a long period in secondary schools
  • Member of the Board of Trustees, Palestine University for Science and Technology, Khan Yunis
  • Was chosen by the Palestinian leadership, in 1992, as a member of the Palestinian negotiating delegation - refugees committee
  • Member of several national organizations and institutions in the West Bank and Gaza Strip
  • Works as a General Director in the Palestinian Ministry of Supplies
    •

Ahmad S. El-Mghanni
Member of the Municipal Council

  • Head of the Rental Committee of the municipal council
  • Has a B. A. degree in Law from Wahran University in Algeria, 1983, an Advocate and a Legal Consultant
  • Member of the Administrative Board of the Gaza Bar Association and the treasurer
  • Member of the Administrative Board of the Sports Union Club in Shuja'iyeh
  • Member of the Founding Committee for the Law of the Palestinian Bar Association for 1995, and the Union of the Palestinian University Graduates

Dr. Maged A. Abu-Ramadan
Member of the Municipal Council

  • Head of the Health and Environment Committee of the municipal council
  • Member of the Information and Documentation Committee of the municipal council
  • General Director in the Palestinian Ministry of Health
  • Deputy of the General Director of the Hospitals General Administration in Gaza Strip
  • Consultant of Ophthalmic Surgery and Treatment in the Palestinian Ministry of Health
  • President and Founder of the Palestinian Ophthalmic Surgery Society (PPOS)
  • Fellow of the Royal Surgery College, Edinburgh - England, (FRCSED)
  • Fellow of the Royal Ophthalmic Surgery College, London (FRCOPHTH)
  • Fellow of the American Academy for Ophthalmic Surgery and Treatment (FAAO)
  • Graduate of the College of Medicine, Ein Shams University - Cairo, 1980
  • A founder member of Gaza Cultural Group
  • Member of the Board of Trustees of the Palestine Children Relief Fund
  • Member of the Administrative Board of the Gaza Program for Psychological Health
  • Member of the Administrative Board of the Gaza Bus Company.
  • Born on 23 December 1955.

Salah Sh. El-Sakka
Member of the Municipal Council

  • Head of the Projects Committee of the municipal council
  • An architect, has a B. Sc. degree in Architecture, 1972
  • Higher studies in Urban and Regional Planning, 1985
  • Director of ANERA in Gaza Strip
  • Vice-president of the Palestinian Development Fund in Jerusalem
  • Member of the Board of Trustees and Vice-president of the Administrative Board of the Arab Thought Forum in Jerusalem
  • Member of the Administrative Board of the Engineers Union for several cycles, one of its founders and head of the Architect Association of the Union
  • Head of the Engineers Union, elected for the cycle of 1978-1989
  • Fellow of the International Union of Architects (VIA)
  • Associated Member of the American Institute of Architects (AIA)
  • Member of the Administrative Committee of the National Book House
  • Coordinator of Habitat International Program for Housing in Palestine

Khaled H. Abdul-Shafi
Member of the Municipal Council

  • Head of the Development Committee of the municipal council
  • Has an M. A. degree in Economics from Berlin University, 1990
  • Director of the United Nations Development Program in Gaza Strip
  • Member of the Board of Trustees of the Arab Thought Forum
  • Member of the Administrative Board of the Development Resource Center and the Women Affairs Center

Maher M. El-Rayyes
Member of the Municipal Council

  • Born on 13 September 1945 in Gaza
  • Has a B. A. degree in Economics and Political Science from Cairo University, May 1968
  • The first Palestinian to get the first place among the graduates of the radio training institution (Egypt, December 1968)
  • Worked with the Qatar Ministry of Information in the field of radio for 5 years
  • Returned to Gaza Strip in 1974 and worked in the field of agricultural production and in business
  • Secretary of the Board of Trustees of Al-Nassr Islamic Model School in Gaza
  • Head of the Information Committee of the municipal council, from 1976 until 1997
  • Participated in many committees which deal with agricultural issues in general and the citrus fruits sector in particular
  • Chief Editor of the "Al-Uloom" Gazan magazine during the period 1978-1984
  • Head of the Administrative Board of the Quraish Development Company since 1997
  • Head of the Administrative Board of the Nahda Group for Investment in Gaza, from September 1998 until present
  • Married with 8 daughters and 3 sons
  • Hobbies include swimming, reading and riding horses
