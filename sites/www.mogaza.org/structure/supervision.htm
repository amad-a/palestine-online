Supervision Department
Director of the Department: Daoud Tarazi, Engineer

Responsibilities:
  • To prepare the work-plan of the department, supervise its implementation and develop the plan whenever needed
  • To examine the claim bills of the contractors before referring them to the director of the storage and supply department, in accordance with the conditions of the tender and the implementation
  • To follow up on the implementation of the projects in accordance with the technical specifications and the tender conditions of the contractors
  • To look for, locally and internationally, funds needed for the engineering projects
  • To attend seminars, conferences and workshop related to the work of the department
  • To present monthly reports on the achievements of the department to the mayor and other departments of the municipality
  • To present reports on the performance of the department employees to the concerned parties.

Sections of the Department

  • Supervision
  • Traffic Engineering
  • Road Maintenance
  • Maintenance of Municipality Facilities
  • Gardens

Section of Supervision
Head of the Section: Taiseer Abu Assi, Engineer

Responsibilities:

  • To supervise the implementation of different projects (water, sewage, roads buildings, rain water drainage, parks, gardens) in accordance with the tenders and plans
  • To prepare the extracts required for giving the payments to the contractors according to what has been implemented
  • To participate in the committees which receive the projects after they are completed
  • To check the buildings which are about to fall and prepare the necessary technical reports

Section of Traffic Engineering
Deputy Director of the Supervision Department and Head of the Section: Khalil Shaqra, Engineer

Responsibilities:

  • To install, work and maintain the equipment and tools needed for controlling traffic like traffic lights, road signs, paint, road crossings
  • To prepare engineering plans for intersections, roads and critical sites
  • To prepare specifications, measures and tenders for the substances and equipment necessary for traffic
  • To collect and organize information and statistics on traffic
    • To participate in traffic work and contact with the authority bodies concerned with traffic works

Section of Road Maintenance
Head of the Section: Munther Al-Ghazali, Engineer

Responsibilities:

  • To maintain the roads covered with asphalt and gravel
  • To maintain dirt roads by using graders and ditch diggers
  • To maintain and repair drainage ditches (water, sewage, rain water)
  • To prepare and repair rain water drainage pipes, metal fences for protecting trees and all blacksmith works
  • To organize transfer operations for the different sections
  • To participate in emergency works.

Section of Maintenance of Municipality Facilities
Head of the Section: Walid Khayyal, Engineer

Responsibilities:

  • To maintain, restore and paint all the municipality buildings and facilities
  • To construct buildings, awnings and walls for the properties of the municipality
  • To install electricity and water for the buildings and facilities of the municipality

Section of Gardens
Head of the Section: Hamzeh Maqat, Engineer

Responsibilities:

  • To establish new gardens and parks in the city
  • To restore, maintain the parks and plant trees in them
  • To maintain the daily cleanliness of parks and prepare them to receive visitors
