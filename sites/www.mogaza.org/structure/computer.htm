The Computer Center
Director of the Center: Hweidi Abu Hweidi, Engineer

Responsibilities:
In addition to the basic duty of the center, it carries out the following duties:

  • To draw strategic plans and prepare studies in order to develop the systems of the municipality and their level of performance
  • To draft policies which define the nature of the computer systems necessary for the municipality and how they are used
  • To develop and install computer networks needed by the municipality
  • To train the municipality staff
  • To maintain anything related to the computers, and the photocopy machines of the municipality
  • To provide the departments and sections of the municipality with technical support
  • To devise technical specifications and prepare tenders to buy things needed by the computer systems and photocopy machines of the municipality and follow up their implementation
  • To be responsible for all the computers, their systems and their attachments, as well as the photocopy machines of the municipality; the center distributes them to the different departments and sections according to the needs of the work and in accordance with the benefits of the municipality.
