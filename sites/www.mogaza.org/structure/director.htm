Rabi' Ayyad Ayyad
General Director of the Municipality

  • Graduated, in 1958, from the College of Business, Political Science and Comparative Governmental Systems in one of the universities of Texas, United States of America
  • Completed higher studies in International Law
  • Worked in the planning administration and industrial production in a number of companies in the United States
  • Director of a branch of an international petroleum company in Algeria in 1971
  • General Director in the Palestinian Ministry of Sports and Youth.
