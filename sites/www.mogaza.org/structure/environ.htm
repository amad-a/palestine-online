Department of Health and Environment
Director of the Department: Abdul Rahim Abu Al-Qumboz, Engineer

Responsibilities:
Direct responsibility of the different sections of the department which include:

  • Sanitation
  • Preventive Health
  • Environmental Protection
  • Environmental Counseling
  • Food Inspection

Section of Sanitation
Head of the Section: Sa'id Al-Dayeh

Responsibilities:

  • To collect garbage from the different parts of the city
  • To send the collected garbage by vehicles to the specified locations and treat it there

Section of Preventive Health
Head of the Section: Kamal Al-Koleh

Responsibilities:

  • To inspect food
  • To inspect drinking water
  • To fight insects and rats
  • To give health licenses to workshops and industries
  • To inspect the complaints of awareness and health counseling and deal with them

Section of Environmental Protection
Head of the Section: Muntaser Shehadeh, Engineer

Responsibilities:

  • To administer and observe lifeguards work at the beaches
  • To beautify and clean the beaches located within the control of the municipality
  • To draw organizing plans for the beach facilities
  • To coordinate with the organization and committees in the joint projects and work which raise the awareness of the environment

Section of Environmental Counseling
Head of the Section: Nawal Zaqqout

Section of Food Inspection and Licenses of Health Professions
Head of the Section: Suhail Abu Abdo
