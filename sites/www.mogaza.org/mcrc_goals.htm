Our Goals

To mobilize youth's local leadership capacities and energies towards activities geared to the development of citizenship and spirit of responsibility.

To provide youth with the means (information, training) for promoting self-sufficiency and a socio-cultural life.

To provide facilities for general information, social and vocational orientation, sports and cultural activities in order to promote the social and economic integration of youth.

To establish working links between governmental institutions, NGOs, private business, in order to act as an official counterpart for these institutions.

To provide general information of training opportunities, on the local and international levels.

To develop social and business oriented leadership skills among Palestinian Youth.

To provide information on Human Rights and civic responsibilities in modern societies through roundtable discussions, with special emphasis to gender issues.

To promote sports & cultural activities for all categories of youth.
