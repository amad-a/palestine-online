Holst Cultural Park
Departments

Computers:

   Holst Park offers computer training by using equipped laboratory which can accommodate up to 20 persons.

Sport:

   In a big hall, highly efficient trainers are engaged in the development of the different sports abilities of the children either in the inside or the outside yard.

Music:

   The music room is equipped with many musical instruments, western & oriental, this include the strings & keyboard instruments training, the vocal training and how to read and write musical notes.

Library:

   The music room is equipped with many musical instruments, western & oriental, this include the strings & keyboard instruments training, the vocal training and how to read and write musical notes.

Theater:

  The theatre is equipped with modern sound and light facilities and can accommodate 200 children. Contract has been made between Gaza Municipality and Theater Day Productions for operating the theater, and this includes theatre performances, drama & animation workshops.

Arts & Crafts:

  Holst Park offers a  multipurpose training for arts and crafts activities. Plastic art and craft training courses are provided by professional artists and craftsmen to help the children how to express their feelings through the art of   using raw materials.
