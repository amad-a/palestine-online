The Community Centers
Arts and Crafts Village
Overview

    The Arts and Crafts Village with its traditional building was designed by Palestinian architect. It was built by Palestinian laborers using mud and local building materials. The project was funded by the UNDP, implemented and supervis-ed by PECDAR as part of its job creation program.

    Municipality of Gaza now runs the village. The Village complex includes an exhibition hall; workshops for hand made crafts, and traditional coffee shop. It has breathtaking azure courtyard, which is its central focus.
