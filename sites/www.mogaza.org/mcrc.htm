What is MCRC?

The MCRC is a multipurpose Community Resource Center directed to address youth issues through providing youth with adequate guidance&orientation information and training that fulfill immediate youth needs aiming by that, at the final stage, to mobilize youth capacities towards activities and to promote the sense of belonging in their societies through promoting their initiatives, creativity and socio-economic integration.

MCRC will also provide training courses that are appropriately selected accordingly to the identified needs which is based on the MCRC vision and the feedback from the participation of the targeted group in the definition of their own interests and needs.
