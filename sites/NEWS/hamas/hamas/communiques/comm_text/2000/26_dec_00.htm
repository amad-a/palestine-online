7- Hamas calls on our Palestinian people to maintain real unity, a unity that would unite ranks in face of usurpers for there should be a form of political, economic and social cohesion so that we would remain in support of each other.

8- Hamas beseeches our beloved people in Al-Quds and its surroundings and all those capable of reaching the Aqsa yards to offer Eid prayers at the holy Aqsa Mosque to deny the Jews the joy of evacuating the Mosque from its soldiers.
