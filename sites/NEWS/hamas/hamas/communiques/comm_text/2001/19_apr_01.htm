Press Release

On the serious injury of one of the Movement's Mujahideen

 Mujahid Khalil Al-Sakani was seriously injured in an armed clash last night with Zionist enemy forces in the Deir Al-Balah area. He is now undergoing an operation in Darul Shifa hospital in Gaza.

The Mujahid is one of the fighters of the Qassam Brigades, military wing of the Islamic Resistance Movement, Hamas.

The Movement prays for Mujahid Khalil Sakani's speedy recovery along with all wounded persons of our brave people. The Movement further affirms its determination to persevere along the road of Jihad and resistance against that enemy until it is ejected from our lands and holy places.

And it is a Jihad until either victory or martyrdom
