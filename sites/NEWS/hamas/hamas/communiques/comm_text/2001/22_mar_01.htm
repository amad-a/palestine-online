1- Bombing the settlement of Dobit in north of Gaza Strip with mortar shells on Friday evening 22/3/2001.

Our proud masses: your Brigades, the Qassam Brigades, pledge to remain your faithful and strong arm with which you fight the arrogant occupiers until they are cleansed out of our usurped lands and sanctities.
