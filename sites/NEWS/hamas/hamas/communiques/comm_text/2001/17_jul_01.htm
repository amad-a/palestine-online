They fired more than five missiles at two civilian houses in which Palestinian citizens were gathered awaiting the release of one of their detained relatives from the occupation prison of Majeddo. The despicable crime resulted in the martyrdom of:

Omar Saadeh, Mohammed Saadeh, Ishac Saadeh and Taha Al-Urouj in addition to wounding 15 women, children and youth who were present in the two houses.
