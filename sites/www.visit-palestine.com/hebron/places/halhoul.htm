Places To Visit

Halhoul

 

A beautiful and fertile village, Halhoul lies 5 km North of Hebron. Abundant vineyards dot the area, producing Hebron's renowned grapes. The Mosque of Nabi Yunis lies just outside the town. According to Muslim tradition, the mosque was built over the grave of the Prophet Jonah.
