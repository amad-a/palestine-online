Nabi Samwil
                   
 
Located 12-km southwest of Ramallah, Nabi Samwil is traditionally held as the site of Prophet Samuel's Tomb. Holy to Christians, Muslims, and Jews, the site consists of a large turreted building and a cellar with the cloth-covered tomb of Samuel. The mountain top village, also called Nabi Samwil, is built around a mosque whose minaret offers an extensive view of the hills of Jerusalem
