THE EDGE

Standing
by the edge of life
Living
by the edge of light
Dreaming
of how to live and love
Living
my dreams at the edge of night.

Wanting you
to be so true
loving your world
is ALL my right

Beyond the pain
beyond your fight
setting you free
is all my plight

Living
at the edge of midnight
Watching
the world watch you fight
Seeking
no more except your right

Your choice to live
to see the light;

TO RUN AWAY FROM
THE EDGE OF NIGHT.
