Samia A. Halaby -- Resume

1936 Born in Al Quds (Jerusalem), Palestine.

EDUCATION

1954-59 University of Cincinnati, Ohio, Bachelor of Science in Design.
1959-60 Michigan State University, E. Lansing, Master of Art in Painting.
1961-63 Indiana University, Bloomington, Master of Fine Arts in Painting.

TEACHING

1962-63 Indiana University, Bloomington, teaching assistant.
1963-64 University of Hawaii, Honolulu, instructor.
1964-66 Kansas City Art Institute, Kansas City, Missouri, assistant professor.
1966 University of Hawaii Summer Session, Honolulu, visiting lecturer.
1967-69 University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Michigan, assistant professor.
1969-72 Indiana University, Bloomington, Indiana, associate professor.
1972-82 Yale University School of Art, New Haven, Connecticut, visiting lecturer, 1972-73, associate professor 1973-76, associate professor adjunct 1976-82.
1985-86 University of Hawai, visiting artist.
1990 University of South Florida, Tampa, visiting professor for one semester.
1989-91 The Cooper Union for the Advancement of Science and Art, New York, adjunct professor part time.
1997 BirZeit University, Palestine, visiting artist for 3 weeks.
