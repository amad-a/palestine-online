Day 64 : Thursday 30.11.2000

Two more Palestinian children were murdered by Israeli occupation soldiers and settlers in the West Bank.

Walid Ahmed Albadan (14 years old) from the village of Tkou’ near Bethlehem, was killed when Israeli soldiers positioned on the rooftop of a multistory building, opened fire on school children outside the village’s school as they were returning home.

Earlier, a Jewish settler from the settlement of Ifrata, south west of Bethlehem, ran over 13-year-old Shadi Ahmed, of the village of Housan, killing him immediately. The settler careened to the roadside where the Palestinian child was awaiting his brother and hit him hardly before fleeing the site. The child bled to death as Israeli occupation soldiers in the area refused to allow a Passing Palestinian car to take him to hospital.
