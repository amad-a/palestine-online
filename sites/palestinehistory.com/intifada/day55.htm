Day 55 : Tuesday 21.11.2000

The Palestinian Authority has welcomed Egypt’s decision to recall its ambassador to Israel in protest against Israeli attacks against the Palestinian people.

Israeli soldiers fired machinegun rounds twice in the past 24 hours at the Muhamadiyya primary school in Hebron. None of the school children was injured.

Here are some photos of today :

Palestinian policemen search through destruction, after Israeli helicopters blasted missiles into Palestinian security targets in the Gaza Strip last night. (Photo 1)

Palestinians rush a Palestinian man injured during clashes with Israeli soldiers to a nearby ambulance on the outskirt of the West Bank town of Ramallah. (Photo 2)

Palestinians carry the body of the Palestinian police officer Mohammad Abu Ubeid, during his funeral in the Gaza Strip. Ubeid was killed by Israeli soldiers gun fire near Gush Katif Jewish settlement in Gaza.
