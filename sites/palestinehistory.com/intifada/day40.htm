Day 40 : Monday 06.11.2000

Three more Palestinians were martyred by Israeli soldiers, Muahmmed Nawwaf al Ta'aban (17 years old) from Deir al-Balah, Wajdi Allam al Hattab (14 year old) from Tulkarm, Muhammed al-Jazzar (17 year old ) died from succumbed injuries he sustained last week.

Over sixty Palestinians were injured in confrontations with the Israeli occupiers, three of them seriously.

Shifa al-Hindi (16 years old), a Palestinian school girl lost her eyesight after she was beaten mercilessly by two Israeli soldiers in East Jerusalem.
