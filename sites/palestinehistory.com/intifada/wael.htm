Wael Imad el Nasheat

A 14 year old Wael Imad el Nasheat , was shot in the head with a live bullet on Sunday, 22 of Oct. 2000 , during clashes between Israeli soldiers and Palestinians stone-throwers at Erez Junction northern Gaza city.

Hundreds of Palestinians carried his body during his funeral in the Jabalya refugee camp in the Gaza Strip .
