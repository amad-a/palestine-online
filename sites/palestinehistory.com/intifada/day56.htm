Day 56 : Wednesday 22.11.2000

Four Palestinian university students civilians were killed and eight were seriously injured when an Israeli tank opened fire on two Palestinian passenger cars in the Gaza Strip. The four are Jamal Abdul Qader, Awni Ismael, Khalil Sha’er and Nayel Shehdeh. The Wife of one of the martyrs was also seriously wounded. The Israeli army prevented Palestinian ambulances from taking the wounded to hospitals in Gaza.

Meanwhile, Israeli helicopter gunships began bombing selective targets in Rafah and Khan Younes.

In a car bomb blast in Khadera north of Tel Aviv, two people killed and at least 25 injured five of them listed in serious condition and one in critical condition.

Two schools were vacated after school children found a small explosive device in the school yard.

The Israeli army dismantled the wholesale vegetable market in Hebron apparently to please the Jewish settlers .

Jewish settlers attacked Arab villages in the central and northern parts of the West Bank, injuring several Palestinian civilians and vandalizing their fields. Near the village of Ein Yabrud, near Ramallah, armed settlers opened fire on a group of Palestinians injuring two of them. Another Palestinian was seriously injured at the village of “Seilet Althaher” near Jenin when armed settlers fired at Palestinians who were harvesting their olives.

An official Jordanian declared that Amman had decided to continue with an earlier decision not to send its ambassador to Tel Aviv until further notice.
