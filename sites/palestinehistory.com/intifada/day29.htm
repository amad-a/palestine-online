Day 29 : Thursday 26.10.2000

In the return march organized by Jordanian people, the security officials use of violence against the demonstrators to stop them from coming close to the occupied territories boarders.

Israeli economy incurred losses reaching up $1,250 billion as a result of the continuing Palestinian Intifada against occupation and oppression as reported by Israel radio.

In a martyr attacker Nabil al-Arair (24 Years old) who was carrying the bomb around his body , approached the side of Kisufim Jewish settlement in the Gaza Strip and blew himself . Number Israeli soldiers has been injured and the attacker was killed . (Photo 1)

In the West Bank city of Ramallah, demonstration continued in the flash points with the occupation soldiers , were number of Palestinian has been injured. (Photo 2)
