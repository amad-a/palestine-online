Al-Aqsa Intifada.. Day 11 - 08.10.2000




Day 11 : Sunday 08.10.2000

On Saturday, Barak issued an ultimatum to Arafat to stop the Iftifada within 48 hours.

Barak is studying a prisoners of war swap where Israel would release Lebanese Islamic leaders, in exchange for three Israeli soldiers captured by Hizbullah at the Shab’a farms, a Lebanese territory occupied by Israel .

Israel PM Ehud Barak has ordered Gaza International Airport shut down , Presedant Arafat is allowed to use the airport , this action has been taken after an Israeli bus carrying Israeli border and customs officer came under fire near Rafah Saturday night.

The UN Security Council approved a resolution condemning the excessive use of force and calling for an immediate resumption of Israeli-Palestinian peace talks with a speedy and objective inquiry on the fighting.

Israeli troops opened fire on school children in Gaza, injuring three of them. Israeli soldiers fired on the children on their way to school in Central Gaza. One of the children was shot in the back, a second in the left foot and a third in the right foot.

Jewish settelers attacked Arab homes in Nazareth ,thorowing stones at Arab homes, fired rubber-coated bullets and tear gas at Palestinans. Five Palestinians were reported injured. Earlier hundreds of Jews encircled the home of Arab Knesset member Azmi Bishara, shouting "death to the Arabs," and "we'll kill all the arabs." During the last ten days clashes between the 1948 Palestinains and the Israeli police left 11 dead and over 300 injured.
