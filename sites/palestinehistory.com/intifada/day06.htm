Al-Aqsa Intifada.. Day 6 - 03.10.2000




Day 6 : Tuesday 03.10.2000

Despite a cease-fire understanding between Israel and the Palestinian Authorities, violent clashes and confrontations between heavily-armed Israeli soldiers and stone-hurling Palestinian protesters continued in several parts of the West Bank and Gaza Strip .

Israeli forces were firing rubber bullets and tear gas shells at schools, in addition to snipers stationed on rooftops opening fire at Palestinian protesters as well as pedestrians.

The total of Palestinian martyrs who were killed by the Zionists since Friday has reached 60, with the injuries topping the 2000 figure.

At least two Palestinian protesters were killed and as many as 15 others were wounded when heavily-armed Israeli occupation soldiers opened fire at a group of Palestinian youths hurling stone at Zionist outposts near to Netzarim Junction in the central part of the Gaza Strip.
