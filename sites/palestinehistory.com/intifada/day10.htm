Al-Aqsa Intifada.. Day 10 - 07.10.2000




Day 10 : Satarday 07.10.2000

The Israeli Army and settlers have withdrawn from Qabr Yusuf, a small Islamic site which was converted into a synagogue several years ago. The site has always been a central friction point between Israeli and Palestinians since it is located in the heart of the Arab town of Nablus. As soon as the site was vacated, Palestinian youths went inside and destroyed the buildings.

The Israeli army shot dead two Palestinian refugees and injured more than twenty others during a refugee protest at Israeli massacres . The two are Hasan Hassanein, 23, of Shatila camp , and Fadi Anas, of Bourj Al Barajneh camp . At least 20 others have been taken to Tyre hospital with injuries.

At least one Palestinian was martyred and number of Palestinians were injured in renewed confrontations between heavily armed Israeli occupation soldiers and Palestinian demonstrators protesting continuing Israeli carnage against the Palestinian people. In Gaza , one Palestinian youth was shot dead at the Shuhada junction in Gaza with fifteen other protesters getting injured. In Tulkarm, at least ten people were injured, four of sustaining gunshot wounds. In Al-Khalil, more than 15 Palestinian protesters were injured in confrontations in Yatta, Samou, Bani Naim and the Fawwar refugee camp. In Jenin, six Palestinians were reported injured.

In Southern Lebanon, Hizbullah forces captured three Israeli soldiers as prisoners near the Shab'a farms. The Israeli government threatened to bomb the Lebanese capital Beirut if the three soldiers were not released.

News reported that the Jewish General Ayora Eiland was the man who issued orders for the killing of the Palestinian child Mohammed al-Durah while trying to hide behind his father in the tragic scene that shook the whole world.
