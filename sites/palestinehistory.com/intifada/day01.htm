Al-Aqsa Intifada.. Day 1 - 28.09.2000




Day 1 : Thursday 28.09.2000

The visit of Sabra and Shatela terrorist Ariel Sharon (Israeli opposition Likud party leader) to Haram al-Sharif of Al-Aqsa Mosque compound caused a lot of tension and anger to the Palestinian worshippers who tried not to allow Sharon from entering the Mosque , and pushed him to short-cut his tour and return back by throwing stones on him and on his security which was more then few hundreds . Israeli riot police and Sharon security clashed with the protested Palestinian and left a big number of them injured .

Sabra and Shatela terrorist Ariel Sharon took this tour to the Al-Aqsa complex to demonstrate that Israel is in control and to give a message to the Palestinian and the Islamic world that Israel will never give back Al-Aqsa Mosque to them and that this is a holy area to Jews where they claim that it has been build on the place of there holy 'Soliman Temple' and they will work to demolish Al-Aqsa and build there 'Temple' on it's place .
