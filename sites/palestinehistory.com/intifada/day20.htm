Day 20 : Tuesday 17.10.2000

Sharm-el-Sheikh summit issued a vaguely-worded statement saying " the two sides agreed to a cease-fire and return to the status that preceded the current wave of violence ". The statement said a fact-finding committee would be formed by the United states in coordination with UN Secretary-General Kufi Annan to establish how the violence started.

Clashes between heavily armed Israeli soldiers and unarmed Palestinian civilians continued throughout the West Bank and Gaza Strip.

In the West Bank, confrontations also continued along the friction lines in Nablus, Ramallah, Hebron, Bethlehem.

40-years old Nabil Al-Abed Khater was killed by Israeli bullet near the Beit Hanun crossing.

Earlier, Farid Masatra, a 28-year-old Palestinian farmer was murdered by Israeli settlers near the village of Beit Furik near Nablus. The settlers armed with machine guns opened fire on a group of Palestinian farmers killing Farid Masatra and injuring seriously three of his relatives.

In Bethlehem and after the funeral of Moayad Jawareesh (14 Years old), Palestinian clashes with the Israeli soldiers. Protesters burned tires and throws stones during the demonstration .(Photo 1)

In Rafah , the Israeli bombarded with rockets Palestinian buildings and at the northern edge of the Gaza Strip Israeli soldiers opened fire on Palestinian laborers, wounding six of them. More then 150 Palestinian protesters were injured in today confrontations.

Clashes between the anger Palestinian demonstrators and the Israeli soldiers broke-out near Erez crossing point . Palestinians throws stones at Israeli soldiers and returned by live bullets and rubber coated steel bullets. (Photo 2)

Number of Palestinian were taken to Gaza Hospital injured by Israeli shooting .

The Israeli tanks advances to the Palestinian village of Beit Jala and fired a heavy machine guns after a shots were fired at Israeli homes in the Jerusalem neighborhood of Gilo , an Israeli soldier was critically wounded in the gunfight.
