Day 38 : Saturday 04.11.2000

Demonstration continued in most towns of Gaza Strip and west Bank , this demonstration lead to clashes and live fire from the Israeli army soldiers against the Palestinian protesters.

A two-month old Palestinian baby , Hind Nedal Ahmed Qweider , was killed in Hebron when Israeli occupation soldiers fired tear gas shells into Palestinian houses in Jabal Jouhar town.

Another 14-year-old Palestinian girl,Ghazala Joudeh, was critically wounded when Israeli soldiers opened fire on school children returning home outside al-Khalil.

The Israeli occupation army pounded several Palestinian towns and villages with rockets, artillery shells, and heavy machinegun fire, inflicting heavy damage to buildings and houses. The heaviest bombardment was concentrated in Hebron were shelled for over six hours, over fifty houses were damaged.
