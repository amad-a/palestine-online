Day 43 : Thursday 09.11.2000

Israeli occupation troops have been firing machinegun fire on Palestinian civilians in Nablus.

Israeli soldiers confiscated the olive crops from many Palestinian farmers in the Nablus area. One farmer was quoted as saying that "they stopped us, seized the harvested crops, and then ordered us not to return our fields."

Israeli helicopter gunships pounded Palestinian targets in the town of Beit Sahur , killing three persons, among them two housewives. A Cobra Helicopter gunship fired a rocket at a civilian car, in which Fatah official Hussein Abayat was riding, killing him instantly, two women died in the incident, Aziza Dannoun (57 years old) and Rahma Shahin (52 years old) .Four more people, including a paramedic and a nurse, were injured, one seriously injured.

In Khan Younis, Muhammed Kamel Shurrab was hit with a bullet in the back, piercing his heart muscle, exiting his chest.
