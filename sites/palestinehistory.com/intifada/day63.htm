Day 63 : Wednesday 29.11.2000

Daily ‘Ha’aretz’ reported that Tel Aviv was holding contacts with the PA via several channels to crystallize a deal that would calm things down topped by a ceasefire and resumption of negotiations. The deal includes confidence-building measures to alleviate sufferings of Palestinians during the fasting month of Ramadan.

Senior Israeli security sources have affirmed that the Palestinian Authority had arrested last night a number of Islamic Resistance Movement, Hamas, activists in Nablus on suspicion of planning armed attacks against Israeli targets.
