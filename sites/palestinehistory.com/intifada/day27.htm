Day 27 : Tuesday 24.10.2000

Israeli soldiers and settlers have killed over 140 Palestinians, including 37 children, and injured as many as 5000 others, since Al-Aqsa intifada began on 28 September.

Two more Palestinians were laid to rest today, one in Gaza, the other in Hebron.

During clashes at the south Gaza strip town of Khan Younis , Palestinians run to escape as Israeli soldiers open fire on them . At least 130 people have been killed in the last three weeks of Al-Aqsa Intifada . (Photo 1)

In Gaza, Iyad Sha’ath (13 year old) died, having succumbed to the serious wounds he sustained last week. He was hit by a live M-16 bullet in the head, causing a rupture in his brain.

In Hebron, Abdulaziz Abu Sneineh (60 years old), was killed by a random Israeli bullet. A 500 mm machinegun bullet hit Abu Sneineh in the head, killing him instantly.

Israel placed its police forces on high alert in anticipation of possible attacks on Israeli targets inside 1948-Palestine (Israel) by Palestinian fighters.

In the outskirt of the West Bank town of Ramallah, and during clashes with Israel army, Number of Palestinian were injured by Israeli troops fire . (Photo 2)

Israel sealed Beit Jalla today based on claims that the Gilo neighborhood on the outskirts of Jerusalem were hit by an automatic weapons from the adjusting Arab village.
