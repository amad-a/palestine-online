Day 31 : Saturday 28.10.2000

Four Palestinians, were martyred by Israeli occupation soldiers in the West Bank and Gaza Strip. As many as 250 people were injured, six sustaining serious gunshot wounds.

Jabre Ahmed al-Mishal (23 years) from Shate’ refugee camp, in the Gaza Strip, Yousef Salem A’waysa (26 years old) from Ramallah, Ahmed Khalil Qasem (28 years old) from Tulkarm and Bashir Salehm Mousa Shalwit (13 years old) from Qalqikya.

In Khan Yunis town south of Gaza Town , clashes between Palestinian stone thrower and he Israeli soldiers caused a big number of injures to the Palestinian side hit by the Israel live bullets and rubber coated bullets. (Photo 1)

In Ramallah city, Palestinians march the streets after the funeral of Ghassan Youssef Salim (27 years old) who was killed in Friday Israeli fire on the demonstrations, today clashes cost more Palestinian injures. (Photo 2)

Israeli army began pounding several Palestinian towns with rockets, artillery shells and heavy machine guns. Among the towns heavily shelled were Beit Sahur, Beit Jala, Ramallah and Hebron.

In Beit Sahur, the YMCA building sustained several direct hits, causing considerable damage, and in Beit Jala Israeli shelling reportedly damaged a number of residential houses.
