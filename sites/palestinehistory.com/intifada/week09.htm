Day 121-126 : From 26.01.2001 To 31.01.2001

Day 121 : 26.01.2001

Palestinian members of the Hamas movement burn a coffin representing the peace process covered by an Israeli flag with , "coordination security is national treason" written on it in Arabic during a demonstration at the Al-Nuseirat refugee camp in the Gaza Strip.


Day 122 : 27.01.2001

A young Palestinian refugee in Khan Younis climbs to the top of a sandbag barricade to look out over an Israeli military checkpoint guarding the Jewish settlement of Gush Katif.


Day 123 : 28.01.2001

Young Palestinian refugees pull a wagon of tomatoes past a sandbag barricade in Khan Younis near an Israeli military checkpoint guarding the Jewish settlement of Gush Katif. The barricades were placed to protect against gunfire in Khan Younis, a main flash point of clashes in the past weeks of violence


Day 124 : 29.01.2001

Israeli soldiers and police inspect the car where an Israeli motorist was shot and killed outside of the West Bank town of A-Ram, north of Jerusalem.


Day 125 : 30.01.2001

Palestinian mourners shout Islamic slogans as they carry the body of Muhammad Majed Abu-Mussa during his funeral ceremonies in the streets of Khan Yunis southern Gaza strip. Palestinian officials said he was carrying concrete blocks to renovate his home.


Day 126 : 31.01.2001

Delegates to a pan-Islamic conference on Jerusalem shout slogans at Israelis in the Fatima Gate border point in southern Lebanon. Some 200 delegates toured the Lebanon-Israel border at the end of their three-day conference in Beirut which voiced support for the Palestinian uprising against Israel.
