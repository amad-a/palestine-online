Al-Aqsa Intifada.. Day 12 - 09.10.2000




Day 12 : Monday 09.10.2000

Jewish settlers abducted a Palestinian farmer Isam Judeh Mustafa Hamed, 47 years old , from his olive orchard, took him to their nearby settlement, bludgeoned him to death with clubs and axes and then set his mutilated body on fire.

Read more..

Two Palestinians were killed and many were injured when a Jewis settlers attacked Arab homes and Businesses in Nazareth in 1948-Palestine Sunday night. Omar Muhammed Akkawi, 42 years old, and Wisam Yazbak, 22, were shot dead , two other Palestinians sustained critical injuries.

Jewish settlers, armed with automatic rifles, axes, and clubs, went to Hebron for the third consecutive night, smashing the windshields of Arab cars and firing heavily on Arab windows.

Jewish settlers continued their attacks on Palestinians within East Jerusalem, shooting live ammunition, rubber coated steel bullets and smashing cars and shops. Two Palestinians were martyred . They are Abdul Hameed Tayeh, of al Mazra'a al Sharkiyya , and Ali Sayel ali Ishak from Azzoun .

It has been reported that the Palestinian Authority had started to prevent Palestinian citizens from throwing stones at Israeli occupation troops. Policemen drew back the demonstrators from the Nitsarim juncture while the Israeli soldiers continued to fire at them injuring 12.

Tanks and armored military vehicles were deployed along the Jerusalem main road near Shufat and Beit Hanina. A number of injuries have been reported. Moreover, Jewish setterls, who have apparently given a free rein to attack unarmed Palestinians, continued to rampage through isolated Arab villages and hamlets.

The Israeli foreign ministry has asked families of its diplomats in the Arab countries to return home immediately.
