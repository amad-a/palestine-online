Day 25 : Sunday 22.10.2000

The Arab summit endorsed two funds to support the Palestinians but refrained from cutting the relations with Israel or criticizing Washington for its blind support to Tel Aviv.

Four Palestinians, including a 14-year-old, were killed by Israeli forces fire in the West Bank and Gaza Strip , bringing the number of dead in three weeks of bloodshed to more the 120.

In Ramallah , Hundreds of Palestinian throws stones during clashes with Israeli forces in Ramallah . (Photo 1)

During a huge funeral in the Jabalya refugee camp in the Gaza Strip, hundreds of Palestinians carry the body of Wael Imad (14 year old) boy who was killed in clashes between Israeli soldiers and Palestinian stone-throwers in Gaza. (Photo 2)Read more..

At Erez checkpoint in the north of the Gaza Strip, Palestinians demostraters run to escape Israeli soldiers fire and teargas during Palestinian-Israeli clashes .

In Ramallah, relatives of 14 year old Palestinian Majid al-Hawamdeh, killed with a live bullets by an Israeli soldiers in the West Bank town of Ramallah , mourn before his funeral in Ramallah.
