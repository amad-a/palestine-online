Day 60 : Sunday 26.11.2000

Israeli occupation troops killed five Palestinians, affiliated with Hamas, near Kalkilya. The five killed are Qusay Zahran (22 years old), Mahmoud Yousef al Adle (23 years old), Muhammed Mansour Odwan (20 years old), Ziad Ghaleb Selmi (17 years old) , and Mahdi Qasem Jaber.

The five martyrs were killed in cold blood and didn’t take part in any armed clash as alleged by the Israeli occupation authorities. The Israelis refused to allow the Palestinian Red Crescent to reach the area.

Elsewhere, one Palestinian Zakariya al Ghoul was killed in the Gaza Strip when Israeli occupation soldiers opened fired on Palestinian protesters near the Mintar Crossing.

Hamas organized a carnival in Nablus to mourn martyr Ibrahim Abdul Karim Bani Odeh one of the commanders of the Qassam Brigades, military wing of Hamas, who was assassinated by Israeli intelligence last Thursday.

Five Israeli occupation soldiers were wounded, two of them seriously when a road-side charge exploded into an Israeli armored vehicle in Gaza.
