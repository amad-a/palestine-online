Day 34 : Tuesday 31.10.2000

Five Palestinian protesters were martyred and over fifty injured during fresh clashes between unarmed Palestinians and heavily armed Zionist occupation soldiers.

The martyrs are: Shadi Oudeh (21 years old), Hazem Abu Daf (17 years old) , Muhammed Hallaf (23 years old) from Gaza, Sa’ed Ibrahim Alzein (21 years old) from Ramallah, and Salim Harbi Mataryeh (25 years old) a policeman from the village of Alkhader near Bethlehem.
