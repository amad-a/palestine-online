Day 100-106 : From 05.01.2001 To 11.01.2001

Day 100 : Friday 05.01.2001

An Israeli soldier on the top of a tank prevents Palestinians from crossing the Netzarim junction near the Netzarim Jewish settlement in the Gaza strip. Israeli tanks closed major roads in the Gaza Strip, effectively cutting the territory in three, and disrupting the lives of more than 1 million Palestinians in the strip.


Day 101 : Satarday 06.01.2001

Palestinian Leader Yasser Arafat walks with Greek Orthodox Metropolitan Cornelius, at the Church of Nativity, during Orthodox Christmas Eve procession in the West Bank town of Bethlehem.


Day 102 : Sunday 07.01.2001

Palestinian Fatoum Khaled bends to kiss her son Amro (10-year-old), in hospital after being shot in the head. Amro was walking home from school in the West Bank town of Ramallah when he was shot by Israel soldiers.


Day 103 : Monday 08.01.2001

Palestinian family members and friends mourn as the body of Abedl hamid Al-Khorati is lowered into a grave at a cemetery near Nuseirat refugee camp in the Gaza Strip. Al-Khorati was killed when Israeli troops fired on his neighboorhood near Netzarim junction.


Day 104 : Tuesday 09.01.2001

Palestinians march through the streets of Nuseirat refugee camp to protest what they say was the indiscriminant killing of Abdel hamid Al-Khorati


Day 105 : Wednesday 10.01.2001

Palestinian mourners carry the body of 70-year-old Ibrahim Abu Maghasaib through a cemetery in the Gaza Strip village of Wadi Salqa. Abu Maghasaib was shot dead by Israeli troops while working on his farm near the Jewish settlement of Kfar Darom.


Day 106 : Thursday 11.01.2001

A Palestinian police officer waves Palestinian traffic through an Israeli army roadblock at Netzarine Junction in the Gaza Strip as an Israeli tank stands guard. After eight days of roadblocks dividing the Gaza Strip, the Israeli Army agreed to allow free traffic again through most of the checkpoints in the territory.
