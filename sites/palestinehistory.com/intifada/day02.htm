Al-Aqsa Intifada.. Day 2 - 29.09.2000




Day 2 : Friday 29.09.2000

Israeli riot police stormed the Al-Aqsa compound and opened fire on worshippers, killing seven Palestinians and wounding 220 in the worst bloodshed in four years .

Violence began during Friday prayers when the compound was filled with 20,000 worshippers. Israeli Officers fired rubber-coated steel pellets and snipers shot live rounds on the prayers just before the end of the pray. Number of Palestinians hit by rubber bullets fell to the ground and were carried away on stretchers.

As news of the clashes spread, riots erupted in other areas of Arab East Jerusalem and the West Bank. In Bethlehem, rocks littered the main road and thick clouds of black smoke from burning tyres rose into the skies.

Sharon's visit was the trigger for the violence and that many casualties were the result of a "pre-planned massacre" by the Israeli security forces.
