Day 72-78 : From 08.12.2000 To 14.12.2000

Day 72 : Friday 08.12.2000

Israeli soldiers and settlers murdered eight Palestinians throughout the West Bank. Four of the murdered are members of the Palestinian Authority general security force who were just getting ready for their Ramadan meal. The bodies of the four, who were manning a roadblock near the village of Jalqamous near Jenin, were badly mutilated. Two more Palestinians were killed in Jerusalem and Bethlehem during marches protesting the Israeli army’s barring of Muslims from reaching Islamic holy places in Jerusalem for the Friday prayers. Another two Palestinians were hacked to death by marauding Jewish settlers around Jerusalem.

Two Jewish settlers living on occupied Arab land were shot dead. A third settler was later shot near Jericho.


Day 73 : Saturday 09.12.2000

The occupation army blockaded major Palestinian towns in the West Bank and in the Gaza Strip preventing Palestinian from entering or leaving their immediate localities, even for an emergency.

A Hebron child Mansour Jaber (12 years old) who was shot and seriously injured by Jewish terrorists near the settlement of Kiryat Arb’a, was listed in critical condition. The child, underwent a five-hour surgery to stop a sever internal hemorrhage caused by three bullet injuries to his lung and liver. The group of armed Jewish settlers from the nearby settlements of Kiryat Arba and Kharsina seized the home of Ata Jaber at the Baka’a neighborhood. The settlers removed furniture and raised the Israeli flag atop the Palestinian home, which has been demolished twice and rebuilt in defiance of the Israeli occupiers. When the Jaber family refused to leave their home, the settlers opened fire on the family members, seriously injuring Mansour in the chest and the belly.


Day 74 : Sunday 10.12.2000

Three Palestinians fell martyrs in the West Bank at the hands of Israeli occupation soldiers and settlers. In Bethlehem Mahmoud Yousef Al Muhghrabi (24 year old) was killed in a resistance mission near Beit Jala, apparently while trying to plant a roadside charge near the tunnel route which bypasses the Arab town. Another Palestinian, a 19 years old, was reportedly killed near the village of Beit Furik near the northern West Bank town of Nablus. And in Hebron, Ahli hospital announced that Ahmed Kawasmi (14 year old) was clinically dead, having been severely injured by gunshot wounds inflicted by Israeli undercover soldiers. The soldiers reportedly captured the child outside the Ali Bakka Mosque in downtown Hebron , dragged him to the ground, and according to an eyewitness one soldier pressed his boot against the child neck and then shot him in the head. Another Hebron child is still fighting for his life in a Jerusalem hospital.

In the Gaza Strip, two Palestinians were laid to rest today. Salim Hamaydeh (12 years old) and Muhammed Judeh (24 year old ) .

311 Palestinian martyrs have fallen since the outbreak of the Aqsa intifada ten weeks ago. The number of Injuries topped 11,000. Around 14 Israeli soldiers and settlers died in the same period.

Israeli premier Ehud Barak tabled his resignation to president Moshe Katzav after admitting that the Palestinian intifada was the reason for his resignation.


Day 75 : Monday 11.12.2000

One Palestinian was killed by Israeli occupation soldiers in the West Bank town of Nablus. Anwar Ahmed Hamran (28 years old) studies at the Alqods Open University. was killed at the Dahiya neighborhood in Nablus as he was returning home from college.

Another Four Palestinians, including a child, were laid to rest in Ramallah, Bethlehem and Al-Khalil. Hilmat Hanani (20 years old) from the village of Beit Furik near Nablus, Hasan Shahin (40 years old) from Bitunya near Ramallah who was hacked to death by Jewish settlers in West Jerusalem on Friday, Mahmoud Al-Mughrabi from Dheishe refugee camp, and Ahmed Kawasmi (14 years old) from Hebron who was killed in a cold-blooded murder in downtown Al-Khalil on Friday.


Day 76 : Tuesday 12.12.2000

Israeli troops assassinated Fatah activist at the village of Khader near Bethlehem.


Day 77 : Wednesday 13.12.2000

Four Palestinians were killed, and as many as 25 others were injured when Israeli occupation troops bombarded the Khan Younis Refugee camp in the southern Gaza Strip. Three Israeli tanks penetrated about 100 meters into the Camp. Palestinian civilians and lightly-armed Palestinian policemen sought to repulse the tanks, setting one tank on fire. At least six Palestinians were seriously injured in the bombardment and more than ten houses were partially damaged or completely destroyed.

The Israeli occupation army assassinated another Palestinian Islamist in Hebron. Undercover Israeli soldiers murdered Abbas Othman Iwewi (26 year old) a shopkeeper, while he was standing outside his store on the Wadi El-Tuffah Street.

Israeli occupation forces riddled a Fatah military cadre Yousef Ahmed Abu Sawwi (28 years old) with 22 bullets while standing in front of his house in the village of Artas near the West Bank city of Bethlehem.


Day 78 : Thursday 14.12.2000

A Palestinian was murdered in cold blood in Gaza. Israeli occupation soldiers opened fire at a minibus which was traveling between Khan Younis and Gaza Town, killing the driver Hani Abu Baker (31 year old). Five passengers in the car were injured, three seriously.
