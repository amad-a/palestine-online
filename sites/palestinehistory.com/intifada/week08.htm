Day 114-120 : From 19.01.2001 To 25.01.2001

Day 114 : 19.01.2001

Palestinian President Yasser Arafat helps carry the coffin of Hisham Mikki, the head of the Palestinian Broadcasting Corporation (PBC) who was shot dead in the Gaza Strip.


Day 115 : 20.01.2001

Palestinian boys try to grab banners with the names of villages destroyed by Israeli soldiers after the 1948 war written in Arabic, during a march for the return of Palestinian refugees, in the West Bank town of Ramallah Saturday.


Day 116 : 21.01.2001

A Palestinian medic, and Israeli soldiers attend to Palestinian Ziad Sharabishi (24 years old), after he was stabbed in the West Bank city of Hebron.


Day 117 : 22.01.2001

Palestinian family members clutch the stretcher holding the body of 15-year-old Mohammed Shareef at the family home in Gaza city. Mohammed was shot dead by Israeli troops near Karni border crossing Sunday evening.


Day 118 : 23.01.2001

Palestinian negotiators Minister of Civil Society Hassan Asfour, left, Security Chief Mohamed Dahlan, third left, and Minister of Information Yasser Abed Rabou, fourth left, take a walk during a break on the third day of their negotiations with Israeli officials at the Hilton hotel in the Egyptian Red Sea resort of Taba.


Day 119 : 24.01.2001

A Palestinian accused of collaborating with the Israeli government hides himself using a blanket for cover inside his closet-sized cell in the fortress-like prison of the West Bank town of Hebron.


Day 120 : 25.01.2001

Palestinian mourners carry the bodies of Safwat Kishta (16 years old), and Halil Elsindy (22 years old) through the streets of Rafah in the Gaza Strip. The two men were killed by Israeli troops on Wednesday around a Jewish settlement near Rafah.
