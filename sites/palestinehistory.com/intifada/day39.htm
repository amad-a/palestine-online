Day 39 : Sunday 05.11.2000

Huge marches were organized in four Palestinian towns in the West Bank to protest the criminal Israeli bombing of Palestinian towns.

At least two Palestinians were killed and over a hundred other protesters were injured by Israeli occupation soldiers bullets .

Marwan Khalil AL-Ghamri (28 years old), was killed when Israeli soldiers opened fire on Palestinian protesters near the Mintar junction near the city of Gaza. Maher al Sa’eidi (16 year old), was killed by the Israeli near the boarders of the Gaza Strip and Israel. Over 50 Palestinians were reported injured in Gaza, seven sustaining serious wounds.

A Jewish settler who found himself in the midst of an Arab town in the West Bank was escorted by Palestinian youths to the nearest Israeli army roadblock.
