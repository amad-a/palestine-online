Day 61 : Monday 27.11.2000

Israeli occupation soldiers are barring Muslims from Jerusalem and the rest of Palestine from reaching the Alaqsa mosque, one of Islam’s holiest shrines, for prayers during the holy month of Ramadan. The Israeli army are allowing only the elderly, those above 45, to reach the mosque.

Here are some photos of today :

A Muslim Palestinian prays on the first day of Ramadan on the dirt barricade of a sealed road, as another Palestinian man carries goods for Ramadan on the road that leads to Bethlehem. The roads leading in and out of Palestinian villages and towns are sealed by a tight Israeli blockade imposed on all of the West Bank and Gaza Strip. (Photo 1)

Palestinians carry two of five bodies of men who were killed by Israeli security forces, at a funeral procession in the West Bank city of Nablus. (Photo 2)
