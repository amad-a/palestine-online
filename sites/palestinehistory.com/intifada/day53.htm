Day 53 : Sunday 19.11.2000

Two Palestinians were shot dead by Israeli soldiers in Gaza Strip. 14-year-old Abdul Rahman Dahshan was killed when Israeli occupation soldiers opened fire on a group of youngsters who were standing opposite an Israeli garrison outpost in Gaza. The children were not involved in any stone throwing. And a 38-year-old Naser Ahmed Najjar Palestinian policeman died of tear-gas inhalation in Khan Younis in southern Gaza.

Israeli army destroyed three houses in the Gaza Strip in what seemed to be a reprisal for Palestinian protests against Jewish military occupation. Israeli army bulldozers demolished the houses located on the Salhuddin Street without any legal cause.

Israeli diplomat Yoram Habibyan was lightly injured in Amman when his car came under fire.
