Day 30 : Friday 27.10.2000

After the Friday pray , demonstrations hit the streets of West Bank towns and Gaza Strip.

Near Jewish Gush Katif settlement in the south Gaza Strip town Khan Younes , Palestinians demonstrators clashed with the Israel soldiers by threw stones and they returned by live bullets , tear gas and rubber coated steel bullets injuring number of them. (Photo 1)

For the third week , Israel border police and the Israeli army prevent the Palestinians less then 40 years old from attending the Friday prayer inside al-Aqsa Mosque. Huge number of Palestinian prayed in the streets of the old city. (Photo 2)

Palestinians march outside the Old City chanting slogans after they were denied entry to attend Friday prayers at al-Aqsa Mosque.
