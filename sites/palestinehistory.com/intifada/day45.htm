Day 45 : Saturday 11.11.2000

Palestinian police officials today handed over a Jewish settler who strayed into the West Bank town of Tulkarm to Israeli police.

The settlers have been responsible for the murder of at least ten Palestinian farmers since the outbreak of the Palestinian intifada on 28 September.

Two Palestinian guerillas were martyred and two occupation soldiers injured one seriously, during armed clash in the southern Gaza Strip. The two Palestinians were killed when the car they were riding came under heavy machinegun fire from a nearby Israeli outpost.

A third Palestinian, 14 year old Basel Abu Qamar, of Gaza, was shot dead by Israeli soldiers near the Beit Hanoun crossing at the northern of the Gaza Strip.

An Israeli soldier died of wounds he sustained during clashes in Bethlehem on Friday.
