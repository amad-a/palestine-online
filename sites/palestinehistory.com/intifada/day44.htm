Day 44 : Friday 10.11.2000

An Israeli policeman was wounded in a blast in East Jerusalem. Initial inquiries indicated that an explosive charge went off near Bab EL-Khalil, an entrance to the walled city. Israel sealed off surrounding streets and carried out a search of the area.

The Israeli soldiers allowed only a small numbers of Muslims to reach al-Aqsa Mosque.

Six Palestinians were martyred during protests and marches following the Friday prayers. The martyrs are Hani Marzouq (35 years old), Ziad Fahmawi (22 years old), Osama Za’aouka, all from Jenin, Ra’ed al Muhtasib (25 years old) from Hebron, and Osama Jarjawi and Muhammed Hamed of the Gaza Strip.

Nearly 200 Palestinians, among them 50 children, have been martyred since al-Aqsa Intifada started on 28 September, and as many as 7000 injured, many seriously.
