Al-Aqsa Intifada.. Day 13 - 10.10.2000




Day 13 : Tuesday 10.10.2000

Palestinian boy Sami Abu Jazar, 12, lies currently connected to a life support machine in the intensive care unit at Al-Shafah hospital in Gaza Strip after being shot in the head by Israeli soldiers in Rafiah.

Read More..

As per PA orders , Palestinian workers reconstructed and painted Joseph's Tomb, in the West bank city of Nablus. The religious shrine was ransacked last Saturday by Palestinians just after Israeli soldiers evacuated the site.

Jewish settlers have been attacking churches and mosques throughout the Jewish state as part of thier campaign against Arabs, Muslims and Christians alike. In Yaffa, Jewish saboteurs "Jami Hasan Bek", an old Mosque on fire. Several Arab homes and busienss were also vandalized by the saboteurs. Thousands of Jaffa Palestinian residents managed in putting out the fire. Few days back a Jewish rolled burning tires into the "Omari Mosque" in the town of Tabariya, setting the mosque in fire.

Jewish settlers closed the Hebron-Jerusalem highway and some other main roads in the occupied territories. Meanwhile, settlers continued to maraud Arab towns and villages in the West Bank, attacking unarmed and helpless Palestinian farmers and often setting fire on their olive fields. Settlers have been implicated in at least four gruesome murders of Palestinians in the past few days.

Barak extended his ultimatum to the Palestinian Authority for another 48 hours to end the Intifadha or else the peace process would be declared dead.
