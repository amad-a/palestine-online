Day 51 : Friday 17.11.2000

Here are some photos of today :

An Israeli policeman argues with a Palestinian man who was denied entry to the al-Aqsa Mosque in Jerusalem's Old City, to attend Friday prayers. Police barred Arab men under the age of 45 from attending the prayers at the mosque, Islam's third holiest shrine. (Photo 1)

A Palestinian youth takes aim at Israeli soldiers as he holds the Muslim Holy Book the Koran during clashes with Israeli soldiers at the Carni crossing in northern Gaza city. (Photo 2)

Israeli riot police face Palestinian Muslims praying outside the Damascus gate on the outskirts of east Jerusalem's Old City. Israeli security forces again banned access to Friday prayers to all Muslim men under 45 at the Al Aqsa Mosque compound.
