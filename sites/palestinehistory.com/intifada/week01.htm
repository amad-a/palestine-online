Day 65-71 : From 01.12.2000 To 07.12.2000
Day 65 : Friday 01.12.2000

Israeli soldiers shot at least four Palestinians, including a child, dead. Muhammad Arja (12 years old), was deliberately murdered by an Israeli soldier near the Salahuddin Gate at the southern tip of the Gaza Strip. Medhat Jadallah (17 years old) succumbed to the serious wounds he had sustained during clashes in Gaza strip. Ibrahim Muhammad Shehadeh from Qalandya refugee camp and Hamzeh Nadi Alhasis of the town of El-Sammou, 20 miles south west of Hebron both has been killed by Israeli army.

The Israeli army strafed several neighborhoods with machinegun rounds at night, causing substantial damage to residential houses and buildings. Israeli occupation gunners fired several artillery shells and heavy machine gun rounds at the Princess Alya hospital in downtown Hebron, inflicting heavy damage and forcing many terrorized patients to seek cover.

The United Nations General Assembly adopted a resolution calling Israel’s imposition of “its laws, jurisdiction and administration” on Jerusalem illegal.


Day 66 : Saturday 02.12.2000

Israeli soldiers shot and killed a Palestinian worker Shehadh Musa Al-Ja’afari as he was doing electric works at a building under construction in Al-Bireh north of Ramallah.


Day 67 : Sunday 03.12.2000

A report prepared by a team of doctors expedited by American human rights’ organization to the occupied lands revealed that more than one thousand Palestinians would suffer from permanent disability as a result of Israeli use of excessive force to quell the Palestinian intifada raging since last 29 September.

Over thirty Palestinians were injured, seven seriously when Israeli occupation soldiers and settlers attacked the village of Housan, a few miles west of Bethlehem. Several Israeli settlers opened fire on mosque-goers soon after sunset, injuring two people seriously.


Day 68 : Monday 04.12.2000

Israeli helicopter gunships strafed two refugee camps near Bethlehem, injuring several refugees and causing substantial damage to property.

An Islamist Palestinian guerrilla Awadh Saleh Salmi (27 years old) was killed and his body was badly mutilated when an explosive device went off near the Mintar Crossing in the Gaza Strip.


Day 69 : Tuesday 05.12.2000

The town of Beit Jala and the neighboring refugee camps of Ayda and al-Azza have been coming under heavy Israeli shelling from the Jewish settlement of Gilo. The civilian neighborhoods were shelled with heavy ammunition and bombs.


Day 70 : Wednesday 06.12.2000

More than 150,000 people took part in the funeral procession of Awad Silmi including Hamas founder Sheikh Ahmed Yassin and all leaders of the Movement in the Gaza Strip.


Day 71 : Thursday 07.12.2000

A Palestinian court in Nablus has sentenced the killer of Hamas guerrilla Ibrahim Bani Odeh to death by firing squad.

Israeli occupation troops fired several tear gas shells at the Sharja School for Girls in Kalkilya, causing as many as fifty children to suffocate. The girls were taken to the main hospital in Kalkilya for treatment of gas-inhalation.
