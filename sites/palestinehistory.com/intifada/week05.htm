Day 93-99 : From 29.12.2000 To 04.01.2001

Day 93 : Friday 29.12.2000

A Palestinian youth pulls a burning tire for a barricade during clashes with Israeli soldiers following a march after the Friday Muslim prayers in the West Bank town of Ramallah.


Day 94 : Satarday 30.12.2000

Palestinian women chant slogans during the funeral ceremony of Mahmoud Nasser at the Beit Hanoun refugee camp, northern Gaza strip. Palestinian policeman Nasser was killed in clashes with Israeli soldiers near the Erez crossing point on Friday.


Day 95 : Sunday 31.12.2000

A Palestinian policeman looks at bullet holes in a car in which Fatah official Thabet Thabet was shot dead in the West Bank town of Tulkarem. Thabet, 49 years old, was shot in the chest and legs as he left his house. Palestinians have blamed Israeli undercover units for Thabet's death three hours after a Palestinian shooting attack killed Binyamin Kahane, settler-son of the anti-Arab Rabbi Meir Kahane.


Day 96 : Monday 01.01.2001

Palestinian mourners carry the body of 11-year-old Muaath abu-Hadwan who was killed by shrapnel overnight in the divided West Bank city of Hebron.


Day 97 : Tuesday 02.01.2001

Palestinians carry the body of Sabri Khader, 52, a farmer who was shot dead by Israeli soldiers while tending his fields near the Jewish settlement of Dugit north of Gaza Strip during his funeral.


Day 98 : Wednesday 03.01.2001

A Palestinian youth uses a sling-shot to throw a rock at Israeli soldiers guarding a bypass road on the outskirts of the West Bank town of Ramallah during clashes.


Day 99 : Thursday 04.01.2001

Israeli bulldozers level Palestinian farms as soldiers in an armored Israeli vehicle look on, near the Jewish settlement of Dugit, northern Gaza strip. Israeli army bulldozers razed about 75 acres of Palestinian agricultural land, including a number of greenhouses and four temporary homes of Palestinian farmers near the Jewish settlement of Dugit.
