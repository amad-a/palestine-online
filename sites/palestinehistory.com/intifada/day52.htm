Day 52 : Saturday 18.11.2000

Yasser Arafat ordered Fatah activists to stop shooting at Israeli occupation targets from Palestinian towns.

One Israeli soldier was killed and two others were injured when a lone Palestinian guerrilla Bahauddin Said (23-year-old) overran an Israeli army outpost opposite the Jewish settlement of Kfar Dorom and opened fired on the soldiers inside their sleeping chamber. The Palestinian guerrilla was martyred by other soldiers. In retaliation, Israeli tanks pounded Force-17 position in Khan Younis, causing considerable damage and injuring four Palestinian soldiers.

Yom-Tov Samia, commander of the Israeli occupation army in Gaza Strip was seen firing from his own gun at demonstrators near Khan Younis. He was also seen scolding his soldiers for using teargas canisters only and for firing at the legs. He urged them to fire live bullets at the higher part of the Palestinian bodies.

Jewish settler families living on occupied West Bank are planning to move to Israel due to the Intifada.
