Day 28 : Wednesday 25.10.2000

30 Palestinian children escaped death when Israeli occupation soldiers in the Gaza Strip fired a tank shell at their school bus. The shell hit the back section of the bus, none of the kids was wounded.

Demonstration continued in all towns of Gaza Strip and West Bank , protesting the Israeli occupation and demanding an end to it .

In the West Bank city of Ramallah, huge number of Palestinian storm the streets in demonstrations, they clashed with the occupation soldiers by throwing stones and in return Israel soldiers reply by live fire and rubber coated steel bullets injuring number of the demonstrators . (Photo 1)

In the south Gaza Strip town Khan Younes near the Gush Katif Jewish settlement, Palestinian youths throwing stones of the Israel soldiers which are protecting the settlement , number of the Palestinian got injured by the rubber bullet and live bullets.

In Gaza a huge demonstration marched the streets after the funeral of 16-year-old boy Nidal Al-Breiki, who was killed during clashes with Israeli soldiers near Erez north of the Gaza Strip. (Photo 2)
