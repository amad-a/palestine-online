Day 58 : Friday 24.11.2000

For another weekend , Palestinians argue with Israeli riot police officers at the entrance to the Al Aqsa mosque compound in Jerusalem's Old City to allow them to enter the Mosque. Israeli security forces banned access to Friday prayers to all Muslim men under the age of 45.

Five Palestinians were martyred and two Israeli occupation soldiers were killed during confrontations throughout the West Bank and the Gaza Strip.

Among the martyrs were two brothers Samir Adel Amer (24 years old) and Nihad Adel Amer (26 years old) from the village of Kufer Kallil near Nablus, who were killed when Israeli tanks bombarded the village on Friday night. One of the martyrs was a physically handicapped boy Ziad Abu Jazar at the southern tip of the Gaza Strip. In Jenin, two boys Aysar Mahmoud and Ghassan al Qura’an were earlier killed by Zionist bullets.

Two Israeli soldiers were killed, one in khan Younis while the other near the Beit Hanun crossing in Gaza.

Thousands of Palestinian citizens offered the Friday prayers at the Ibrahimi Mosque in Khaleel (Hebron) for the first time in two months due to its closure by Zionist occupation authorities.
