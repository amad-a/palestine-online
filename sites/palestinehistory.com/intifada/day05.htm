Al-Aqsa Intifada.. Day 5 - 02.10.2000



Day 5 : Monday 02.10.2000

Violent confrontations between heavily armed Israeli soldiers and unarmed Palestinian protesters continued for the fifth consecutive day , with the Israeli army using heavy machine guns and anti-Tank missiles in a desperate effort to quell Palestinian anger over the desecration of Muslim holy places in Jerusalem by Israeli opposition leader Ariel Sharon.

The Intifada continue in almost all the main cities of Gaza Strip and the West Bank . The latest reports showed that the total from Sunday's events reached 12 martyrs and the injuries 430 , at least 22 of the wounded sustained gunshot wounds in the head and the chest, and their condition was listed as between serious and critical.

In Gaza Strip, Israeli helicopter gunships have attacked Palestinian police positions. The attack helicopters bombarded three cars at the Netzarim Junction, killing two policemen and injuring others. The use of the Israeli air force against Palestinian protesters is viewed as a dangerous escalation aimed at subjugating the evolving Palestinian intifada.

Moreover, various manifestations of unrest continued to take place across the Green Line (1948-Palestine), with Palestinian youths hurling stones on Israeli soldiers and blocking entrances of Palestinian towns. At least two Palestinians from the 1,200,000 strong Palestinian community in the Jewish state were killed by Zionist bullets. Over a hundred people were reported injured, including ten sustaining seriously injuries.
