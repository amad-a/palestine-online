Mister President, we strongly appeal to you to support the rights of the Palestinian nation and to immediately interfere to stop the growing chances of a true human disaster on the medical, social and political levels which endanger the chances of PEACE in the future and lead the area for more tension and a wider conflict. Therefore, the Palestinian nation has the legal, human, civil and legitimate rights to defend their own existence, lands, properties and their nationhood similar to any other nation under illegal military occupation in other parts of the world, using all legitimate methods to defend themselves. Remember Mister President, that all Arab Nations selected the peace and the last proof is the Latest Arab Peace Proposal.

We demand that you have your own Peace Proposal based on the United Nation and International Law to force Israel to accept the Arab Peace Proposal and to implement it on a fixed, short-term and compulsory schedule under supervision of independent international monitoring and control.

Millions of free people around the world are appealing to you to stand side by side of the innocent people of Palestine and to stop the ethnic cleansing war ongoing by the Israel army against them for the last days.
