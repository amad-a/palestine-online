Palestinian child center in Ramallah

Israeli soldiers are deliberately targeting Palestinian children, aiming to kill, maim and disable as many of them as possible, according to a report by a Palestinian child center in Ramallah on the West Bank.

Director of the Palestinian Happy Child Center, Dr. Jumama Audeh, said the high rate of casualty among children leaves no doubt as to what the occupation army is up to.

"They want to kill, maim, paralyze and disable an entire generation," she said in a report published Monday.

Audeh pointed out that over 25 Palestinian children were killed and over 500 other children were wounded in the ongoing Palestinian uprising.

Audeh said that the overall percentage of wounds to the upper parts of the body (heart and head) was initially 50,2%, and has gone up to 83.2 % in the later period. The use of live ammunition, she said rose from 20,2% to 57,7%, and now to 82%.

The report furnished the additional following additional figures: 44% of casualties are now injured in the chest and 43% in the head (for a combined total of 87%). Moreover, 18% of all wounded are in a state of clinical death, critically wounded, paraplegic or quadriplegic.

Furthermore, 20% of all the wounded are likely to remain saddled with a permanent disability; the average age of all martyrs or wounded persons is 21 years.
