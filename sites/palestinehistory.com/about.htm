Palestine - Home of history

Palestine - Home of history , is a site project dedicated to the current history of Palestine from 1900 and up to today . We in this project aiming to have a ready reference site for all information about the background and current history of the land of Palestine and the conflict on it between Arab and Jews .

This site is an on-line encyclopedia about Palestine .

There is a lot to find your self in this site , kindly revisit and inform your friends about it . Your visit will make us more interested to develop the site and add more information .
