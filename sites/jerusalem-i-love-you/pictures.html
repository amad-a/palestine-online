The Blood Desire

On the 13th of January 2001 , the Nazi Zionist soldiers attacked Al-Khalil (Hebron) which is supposedly ruled by the PNA and broke into a peaceful restaurant, to shoot Shaker Hassona (23 years old) in the abdomen, chest and neck. After the martyr was killed, the soldiers of the devil dragged the body of the martyr on the street drawing a thick long line of the blood, among the cheering of the Jewish settlers who gave candies to the brave soldiers who shot the totally unarmed Palestinian young man.

Hassona, was not shot in an operation against the Zionist soldiers, nor in a demonstration. He was shot in a restaurant while he was having his lunch.
