I wanted to send you all my support and love for this magnificent newspaper, I was always reading the Arabic newspaper which is published in Jerusalem until I found your magazine, since then I started to read yours daily almost.
By the way I am a Palestinian woman and I have been living in Iceland for five years. I am a mother of a daughter by the name of Falasteen, in another way Falasteen is with me where ever I go, and I would like to know if it is possible to share your magazine with some articles about the woman or anything else.

amal
