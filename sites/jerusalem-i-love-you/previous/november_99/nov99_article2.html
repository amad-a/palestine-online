ARABIC OR ISLAMIC UNITY???

Abdulghani Saffori

 

I would like to ask a question:  What do you prefer, the Arabic unity, or the Islamic unity??? This question has been asked by the millions of the Arabic people all around the world. This subject is pretty hard to discuss, but we will have a time to discuss it and take it very serious. I would like to define the Arabic Unity.

Arabic unity is the unity between all the Arabic countries, from the east to the west, from BILAD AL SHAM to Mauritania and AL MAGHRIB AL ARABI.

Unity between all these countries is very easy, but the Arabic leaders don't respect it because may be a country will loose some economic aspects.

We have to unite with each other. Europe is about to be one country, same kind of money, same politics, same socials, same everything. Why can't we do like them??? This is the question. Arabic Unity is much easier than Islamic unity. I will tell you why. Because there are a lot of Arab Christians, Drooz, or another religion.

Because of those religions. Finally, I have to say that we have to unite with each other to be more powerful, and nobody can talk about us because we are so strong to do so.

"WE MUST UNITE WITH EACH OTHER."
