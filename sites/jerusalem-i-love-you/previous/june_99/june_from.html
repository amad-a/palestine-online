Welcome to the first issue of "Jerusalem I Love You". The third site on the Internet by Falasteen Association, in the USA, "Falasteen", and "Alaseer" in Arabic.

The Idea of this site stemmed from our Arab American readers who loved our Idea but could not read Arabic.

Our site is 100% independent and does not belong to any party or political movement or government.

We believe in the freedom of speech, so our sites are open for all opinions and thoughts without any objection. We encourage all readers to write to us so we can publish their opinions on the Internet. Please provide us with your name and the country you are living in. Our site will be a monthly magazine and hopefully in the future will be weekly.

In the name of all our staff we like to thank Mr George Kandalaft, journalist and web developper, for the time and effort he spent with us to design this site, he was patient and helpful and deserve the warmest greetings we can send.
