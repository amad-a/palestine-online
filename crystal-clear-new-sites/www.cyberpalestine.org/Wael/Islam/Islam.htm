Here are some of favorite links to Islam-related websites with a brief description of each. Of course, I must first mention our local MSA's website first: MSA at the University of Kentucky.

CyberMuslim Information Collective Provides information and resources regarding Islam worldwide. Offers links to the HyperQur'an project. Includes information on Islamic culture, schools, computing services, bookstores , and digital activism.

Ibrahim Shafi's Page in Islam - Rich in links focusing on the cornerstones of Islamic religious life. Connects to Muslim organizations. texts, ftp, Gopher sites, newsgroups, and other resources. Also offers links to Muslim countries. Great starting point for the study of Islam.

Islam A Shi'ite site. Many introductory essays. Includes the Shi'ite encyclopedia and information about the Ahlul Bayt. Features a daily verse from the Qur'an and a daily saying that represents Islamic principles.

Islam's Home Page A valuable Islamic resource containing articles about various Islamic issues incluiding the Renaissance of Islam, women in Islam, and Jesus in Islam. An English translation of the Holy Qur'an and several Islamic prayers can be found at this site, as well as a collection of Islamic images.

Islamic Gateway Excellent Islamic site in Great Britain.

Islamic Society of Wichita For the serious researcher. Multiple databases for the Qur'an, mosques, prayer timings, and numerous other topics of interest to Muslims. International in flavor.

Muslim Students Association News - MSANEWS A HUGE collection of of news and resources related to Islam.

Online Islamic Bookstore Provides information about the books, tapes, and software. Offers links to Islamic sites and book reviews of important books. "To encourage the Muslim community to read again. To assert the importance of spiritual knowledege especially in this modern age."

Salaam Ailaikum Contains Islamic and social justice poetry, articles, links, and stories. Also contains several articles by the Islamic author, the late Dr. Ali Shariati.

University of Essex (UK) Islamic Society Web Pages An excellent site for Islamic resources in Great Britain.

WAMY IslamNet (World Assembly of Muslim Youth) Presents links of interest to students and practitioners of Islam. Includes sounds, magazines, and Fiqeh database online, among other resources.
