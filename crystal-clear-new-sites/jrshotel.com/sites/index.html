The Jerusalem Hotel is situated in the heart of Jerusalem. It is less than a minute walk to the Old City and few minutes away from both Salah Eddin and Ben Yehuda streets, which are the main shopping areas of East and West Jerusalem, respectively. To get to the hotel from the central bus station in Jerusalem, take the 27 bus, which ends right in front of the hotel. The easiest and probably the most reasonable way to get to the hotel from the airport is via the Nesher collective taxi service.
