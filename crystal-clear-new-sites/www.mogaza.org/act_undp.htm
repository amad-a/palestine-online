    This program is one of the activities of the UNDP to support the Palestinian people. The UNDP has assisted in constructing variety of projects such as:
Developing roads, constructing water and wastewater, constructing schools, hospitals and creating job opportunities and building up the capacity of the Palestinian institutions. This program had led to raise the people standard living and to improve the economic and social aspects for the people. The estimated cost of the projects, which executed under this program is $9,000,000.
