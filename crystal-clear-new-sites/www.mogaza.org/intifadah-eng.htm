The Fallacy of the so-called Israeli Democracy:

A Badge of Shame!

Municipality of Gaza is extremely appalled by the barbarian and brutal Israeli practices against the innocent Palestinian civilians.

Since the outbreak of the bloodiest massacre of Al Aqsa Mosque, which was invoked by the provocative visit of the notorious terrorist Sharon to Al Aqsa Holy shrine. The Municipality of Gaza represented by Mayor Aown shawa, the Municipal Council and senior staff members, has been closely and continuously following up the accelerating incidents of confrontations between the innocent and unarmed palestinian civilians and Israeli army equipped with the most sophisticated weapons of mass killing. The Municipality issued a series of statements denouncing the Israeli forces of occupation for killing and injuring thousands of Palestinians by using internationally banned weapons. The municipal council, headed by the Mayor, appealed to the world community to stand beside the Palestinian people in order to put an end to the most brutal and fascist campaign of killing and annihilation of the Palestinian people.

The Mayor Shawa calls for pressure on Israel

Gaza Municipality represented by the Mayor and the members of the municipal council participated in marches of protest against the ugly crime committed by the Israelis against our people. The Mayor called for the European Union, the United Nations and the Security Council to exercise pressure on Israel in order to stop the crime perpetrated against the Palestinian people. Being the president of APLA, association of Palestinian Local Authorities, Mr. Shawa made contacts with several town organizations and some mayors in Europe. He explained to them the situation on the ground and informed them about the massacres committed by the Israelis with cold blood. In return, Gaza Municipality received massages of solidarity from many European cities and organizations.

The Mayor visits the injured and pays tribute to martyrs

The Mayor and the members of the municipal councils have visited the wounded people at Shifa hospital. Regardless of their injuries that vindicated the brutality of the Israeli soldiers, the wounded showed high morale and pledged to continue their struggle until the Palestinian State with Jerusalem as its capital is established. The Mayor and the members of the municipal council were very shocked by the severe injuries of some young men; injuries that were caused by internationally banned weapons and at the same time showed the randomness of shooting. The Municipality of Gaza has payed tribute to the martyrs of the current Intifada against the Israeli occupation.

Israel is not interested in genuine peace but rather in bloodshed

In the sequence of his comments on the recent massacres committed against the Palestinians, Mayor Shawa said that Israel does not desire peace but rather seeks to impose certain solutions that do not accord with international legitimacy and ignore the feelings of the Muslim world. Sharon would not have desecrated Al Aqsa without the knowledge and the consent of Barak who supported the visit by sending three thousand soldiers to protect him. The Mayor affirmed that Israel would never comply with the International legitimacy without the pressure of the international community, he was skeptical of the American role because he believes that USA completely adopts the Israeli stand, therefore, it would not be a fair judge.

The Municipality of Gaza declares emergency

To cope with the newly emergent situations, the Municipality of Gaza declared a state of emergency .   All the departments and the employees have been on alert in order to serve the public around the clock and to handle every rising problem on the spot. On the other hand, the employees of the municipality hurried to help their brothers by donating their blood and giving first aid to the wounded.
