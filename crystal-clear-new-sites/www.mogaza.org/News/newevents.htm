Mrs. Soha Arafat is gratified by " The Italian Week for Peace in Gaza "

Gaza City has witnessed a day of social and political initiatives within the " Italian week in Palestine for peace in the Middle-East”. More than one hundred official and popular Italian institutions contributed to this week. The project was promoted by the Italians with the cooperation of the Palestinian institutions and municipalities. The Italian initiatives in Gaza have been marked by a great deal of excitement and positive impact. Mrs. Soha Arafat, the president's spouse, participated in the activities of this day. She was highly impressed by the distinctive Italian and Palestinian relationship on both levels, the official and the popular. Mrs. Soha deeply thanked the Italians for their support to the Palestinian cause.

 

 

Local Action for Urban Development: The experience of the European and the Palestinian Local Authorities and their National Organizations

Gaza City has hosted a one-day conference for urban development. The conference which was held at Rashad Al Shawa Center on Sept. 2nd was organized by APLA, the Association of Local Palestinian Authorities in conjunction with the Italian Committee at UTO, the United Town Organization. The European participants have represented 35 municipalities, provinces and governorates from France, Belgium, Italy, Spain and Portugal. The Palestinian side was represented by 22 municipalities. The conference has achieved numerous aims. The most important of which is laying a mechanism where by cooperation between the European and the Palestinian municipalities, mainly in the field of urban development and planning, is fostered. Reinforcing the financial potentialities of the Palestinian municipalities was another major concern of the conference. On political level, the conference has stressed the Palestinian rights of self-determination and the establishment of the imminent Palestinian Sate with East Jerusalem as its capital.

A successful visit to Dunkirk has been concluded

Mayor Aown Shawa returned from Dunkirk after a successful official visit. It is worth mentioning that Gaza City has been affiliated to the French City, Dunkirk by a twinship agreement Since 1995. Common fields of cooperation between the two cities were discussed during the visit.

 

 

The Municipality of Gaza hosts a French mass media delegation

A large French mass media delegation has paid a three- days visit to the Municipality of Gaza. The visit was organized by the Arab World Institute in Paris in coordination with the Palestinian embassy in France. During their visit, the members of the French delegation met with Mayor Aown Shawa who gave them a detailed explaination about the current political and economic situation in the region. He stressed the fact that the Israelis, notwithstanding the peace process, are still practicing the most aggressive and coercive means against the Palestinian people. The Mayor explained to the delegation the situation of Gaza City prior and after the coming of the Palestinian National Authority. He surveyed some of the development projects that have been executed in Gaza sine the coming of PNA. The French media men were highly impressed by the meeting with the Mayor of Gaza and thanked him for the warm reception. During the visit, the Municipality of Gaza organized for the visiting delegation a tour around Gaza City. The guests visited some historic and antiquity sites in Gaza city. They also had the opportunity to visit the sites of some completed and ongoing projects. In return, the French TV team working for the French News Network made a comprehensive coverage of the situation in the city and interviewed some high ranking Palestinians from different ministries.

Mayor Shawa calls for an active and balanced European role

During his meeting with the delegation of the young European volunteers in Gaza, Mayor Aown Shawa calls for a more active political and economic European role in the region. A role, which counters balance the wholly aligned American one. Mayor Shawa thinks Europe is held responsible morally for the Palestinian people, He added that it is the duty of the European Community to render every possible assistance to the Palestinians.

 

 

Booklets for tourists in Gaza

The Municipality of Gaza has recently issued three versions of a tourist booklets. These were written in three languages: Arabic, French, and English. The municipality strongly believes that such a booklet would help to sustain tourism in Gaza and in effect would be indispensable for tourists. The booklet contains essential background information about religious and antiquity sites. It also contains a list of telephone numbers of hospitals, police and emergency centers. A synopsis about Municipality of Gaza was introduced in the booklet.

The Municipality of Gaza concludes El Rasheed Street Development Project

Gaza Municipality has just finalized the development of Rasheed Street. This street, which is parallel to the beach, boasts itself for its tourist facilities such as fancy hotels and restaurants; the project was jointly funded by the UNDP, the Municipality of Gaza and some donations received from tourist establishments. It is worth mentioning that this project is one of the many projects executed by the Gaza Municipality under the national and the comprehensive project, “ Towards clean and Beautiful Gaza “.
