The 250m2 Gallery is inspired by traditional Islamic architecture. Constructed as a house, the spaces for exhibition are inspired by the traditional ”Youk”.

   The walls and ceiling have the form of arches, vaults and cupolas, and the traditional interior patio is also reproduced.

   The Gallery hosts, on a rotating basis, the work of different Palestinian and foreign artists, ranging from traditional to contemporary arts.
