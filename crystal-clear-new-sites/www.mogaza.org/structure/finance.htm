Financial Department
Director of the Financial Department: Mohammad Subhi Zeiniddin

Responsibilities:

  • To have complete responsibility of the different sections and units of the financial department
  • To have direct administrative supervision of all the department employees, to guide them, follow up their work, and distribute the daily duties in a way which guarantees the safety of the daily work
  • To endorse expenditure and receipt vouchers of the ordinary and development budgets
  • To participate in the committees of financial nature (committee of opening envelopes, tender, auction) and other committees
  • To supervise the application and implementation of financial laws, regulations and instructions
  • To present the project of the normal budget to the Mayor and the municipal council for approval
  • To use the money and the properties of the municipality in the best possible manner
  • To supervise the application of the accounting system and the accounting principles on the daily financial work of the financial administration
  • To present recommendations and suggestions proper for developing the work and increasing the revenues of the municipality
  • To coordinate the work of the financial department with all the departments and sections of the municipality in order to perform the work at the most possible efficient and effective level
  • To evaluate the performance of the financial department employees and present the evaluation to the Mayor
