Department of Urban Planning and Organizing
Director of the Department: Fuad Al-Shurafa, Engineer

Responsibilities:

To supervise daily and directly the different sections of the department including:

  • Licensing and Construction Observation
  • Urban Planning
  • Survey
  • Vocations and Advertising
  • Naming and Numbering
  • Systems of Geographical Information

Section of Licensing and Construction Observation
Head of the Section: Nahed I'zeizeh, Engineer

Responsibilities:

  • To give building licenses through the local planning committee
  • To give works permits (water and electricity services and vocation licenses)
  • To observe buildings and give warnings to unlicensed buildings, or which violate the licensing conditions
  • To study the problems of the residents and provide suitable solutions

Section of Urban Planning
Head of the Section: Majed Abu Rahmeh, Engineer

Responsibilities:

  • To find out the public locations before obtaining building licenses
  • To prepare organizing plans for the streets, to conduct studies necessary for the planning of the areas, and to give suggestions
  • To participate in the local planning committee
  • To prepare projects for land reparceling
  • To participate in solving the planning problems of the residents

Section of Survey
Head of the Section: Mustafa Zu'rob, Engineer

Responsibilities:

  • To prepare budgets for leveling the land ----road leveling????? in preparation for implementing municipal projects
  • To define the borders of land plots in order to prevent using parts of the streets
  • To submit maps for unparceled plots of land
  • To revise reparceling projects
  • To answer the complaints of the residents

Section of Vocations and Advertising
Head of the Section: Ali Radwan

Responsibilities:

  • To issue new licenses and open files for them in the section
  • To renew licenses at the beginning of every year
  • To do modifications on vocations under the will of the owners
  • To check stores through daily field visits and issue warnings
  • To follow up the complaints of the residents and do what is required to solve them

Section of Naming and Numbering
Head of the Section: Imad Jaradeh, Engineer

Responsibilities:

  • To draw streets and plots on detailed plans and submit them to the naming and numbering committee
  • To conduct field survey on the sides of the streets in order to fix the names and numbers on them
  • To prepare curriculum vitae for the Moslem scholars, famous people, and martyrs whose names are to be used on the streets
  • To prepare technical specifications for the signs and numbers

Section of Geographical Information Systems
Head of the Section: Ahmad Sweilem, Engineer

Responsibilities:

  • To conduct field survey for all the buildings in Gaza city in order to collect information (name of the owner, number of floors, state of the building, the material of the roof and walls)
  • To modernize and modify aerial maps in accordance with the previous survey
  • To start an information network for the infrastructure (sewage, water, electricity). and add it to the aerial map
  • To prepare tourist maps of Gaza city
