Welcome









Mr. Nasri M. Khayal

It's my pleasure to welcome you at our web site which was initiated recently to provide you with information regarding the City of Gaza.

Almost seven years ago, the Israeli occupying forces left Gaza and parts of the Gaza Strip and the West Bank, as part of the peace process which brought the return of the Palestinian National Authority under the leadership of President Yasser Arafat.

Much have been achieved during these seven years, and the city enjoys a healthy environment, a beautiful beach and rehabilitated neighborhoods. Quite a change from the dark days of the Israeli occupation.

However, the Gaza Strip still suffers from the Israeli blockade, which deprives the Palestinians from free movement and trade. This is the major factor causing high rate of unemployment and poverty, as part of the official Israeli policy.

Like other people of the world, the Palestinians have their dreams of prosperity, stability and freedom. This can be achieved only when justice and mutul respect prevails between the parties.

We look to the future with optimism to establish our Palestinian independent state with Jerusalem as our Capital.

 

Thank you again for your interest and support.

 

 

                                                                                            Nasri Khayal

                                                                                                                             1st May, 2002

  • A graduate in Economics and Business Administration from the American University of Cairo (AUC).

  • President of the Association of Palestinian Local Authorities (APLA) , (1998-2001).

  • President of Delegate, United Towns Organization (UTO).
