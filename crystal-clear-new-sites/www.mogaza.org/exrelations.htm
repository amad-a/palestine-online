Mr.Elashi

The External Relations Department
Director:  Basil J. Elashi


It is divided into two sections:
1- International Relations.
2- Public Relations Section.

 

Goals &  Objectives:

  • Creating a new civilized image for Gaza city on the international level.

  • Helping in the fund raising for vital projects in Gaza city.

  • Encouraging the process of exchanging information & experiences in various aspects between Gaza Municipality, its twin cities, and other international organizations, especially those working in the field of local authorities.

 

Duties:

  • The follow up of cooperation aspects with cities twinned with Gaza.

  • The creation of new relations with International organizations, institutions & cities through bilateral exchange relations with Gaza Municipality.

  • Arranging the Mayor’s visits to foreign  countries & to facilitate the participation of Mayor in international conferences.

  • Arranging the visits of foreign delegations to Gaza city & Gaza  Municipality.

  • The facilitation of communication with foreign embassies & consulates, and also with International agencies & organizations working in Palestine, this includes coordination to make regular meetings between the Mayor, foreign ambassadors & CGs.

  • The organization of meetings of foreign delegations with the Mayor.

  • The follow up of relations between Gaza Municipality & International organizations in which the city of Gaza is  a member.

  • The preparation of brochures, booklets & other communication materials which facilitate the mission of the External Relations of Gaza Municipality.

  • The coordination with cultural centers attached to Gaza Municipality as per the needs of those centers.

 


Mr. Morad

Public Relations Section:
Head of the Section: Wael Moneer Morad

Responsibilities:

  • To promote the relations between the municipality and those who deal with it, residents and organizations.

  • To receive delegations and organize visits.

  • To facilitate travel applications and permits necessary for the municipality employee.

  • Designs all the printed materials of the Municipality.

  • Designs and updates the web site of the Municipality.

  • Disseminating planned messages through media to further the Municipality’s interest; Responding to public concerns; Informing and motivating the Municipality’s employees through special events and activities; Working with mass media in seeking publicity or responding to their interest in the Municipality; Demonstrating through written and multimedia materials particular municipal or community needs for the purpose of enlisting financial contributions and/or grants.

  • Issuing press releases and official tenders and announcements; Arranging official interviews for media; Arranging visits of international delegations (travel, Accommodations, tours programs, etc); Organizing official Municipality functions, receptions, cultural and educational events; Supervising Municipality support; Services staff; Dealing with public complaints.
