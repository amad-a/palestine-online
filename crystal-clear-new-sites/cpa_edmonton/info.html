Following the recent tragic events in New York and D.C. on September 11,
there has been a disturbing rise nationwide in the number of hate crimes
directed toward individuals and communities of Arab, Middle Eastern and
South Asian descent as well as towards those of Muslim faith. There has
been consistent media underreporting of such events. CESR believes that
human rights violations such as these must be documented and condemned.
Below are several important resources regarding hate crimes stemming
from the events of September 11.

The Council on American-Islamic Relations has set up an on-line form for
reporting of anti-Muslim and anti-Arab hate crimes.
The address is http://www.cair-net.org/ireport/.

The American-Arab Anti-Discrimination Committee is a leading
organization dedicated to defending
the civil rights of people of
Arab descent                        .
