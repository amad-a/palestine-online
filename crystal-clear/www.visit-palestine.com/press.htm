Press Release

                Recently the Israeli Army and Police has prevented tourists to enter Palestinian towns and villages and the Israeli Ministry of Tourism has distributed letters to the local and international tour operators declaring that the Palestinian areas are closed for tourists especially Bethlehem and Jericho.

Despite the current political situation not a single incident has been registered against tourist, tourists� buses or guides in the Palestinian areas. They are protected by the Palestinian People and Authority without any distinction between Palestinian and Israeli Companies and individuals.
