                  JERICHO 

                  Nabi Musa 

According to Muslim tradition, Nabi Musa is where Moses is buried. The tomb of Nabi Musa (the prophet Moses) has been a site of annual pilgrimage since the time of Salah al-Din. The main body of the present shrine, however was built by Mamluk Sultan Baybars in 1269. You can see a beautiful and serene mosque, with a white domed roof, glazed green mihrab (prayer niche) and painted green wooden minbar (pulpit) .
