Oak Of Abraham  

An oak tree 2 km west of Hebron marks the legendary site where Abraham pitched his tent. Excavations in 1926-28 revealed a Herodian enclosure with a well in its southwestern corner. Until recently, pilgrims used to peel pieces of the trunk for good luck. Now, however, the Russian Orthodox Church who owns the site and the nearby monastery has wrapped the trunk with steel braces for protection.
