Jerusalem Links

Jerusalemites.org is an Internet site, which focuses on the human dimension of Palestinian history, in the hope of helping to build a growing database of documented, credible information for posterity.

This online medium deals with such topics as Palestinian history, culture, and attachment to their land, as well as Israeli crimes against humanity. The site addresses the importance of Jerusalem to Christianity and Islam and has a growing Memoirs page, comprising oral accounts told by the people who witnessed events as they unfolded.

Its objective is to reinforce Palestinian presence and to portray daily life in Jerusalem and Palestine as a whole. Jerusalemites intend over a period of time to tell the world the Palestinian people's story largely, but not exclusively, from a human perspective. Please see our Mission Statement for more information.
