Hanthala Palestine started as a group working through internet and mainly by providing news, comments, articles, reports, issues which you do not very often will find in the mainstream newspapers. Moreover, we started discussing means and aims of Hanthala. What are our conditions where it comes to human rights, Palestine and our stands on specific issues. Not only Palestinians living in Palestine and Palestinians in the diaspora were connected, also internationals, whether they have come to Palestine to study, work, or participating in summercamps.
