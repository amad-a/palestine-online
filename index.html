Palestine Online is a collection of webpages created by Palestinians in Palestine and abroad, as well as allies moved(?) by the Palestinian Cause. This collection spans:
  • personal pages created on geocities, featuring 3D gifs, biographies and personal accounts of living under occupation
  • interactive educational content including virtual tours of al aqsa and a visual timeline of Palestinian History
  • "hub" sites containing links to other websites relating to Palestinian news, entertainment, etc.
  • tourism websites highlighting cities and attractions
  • Artist homepages and blogs
